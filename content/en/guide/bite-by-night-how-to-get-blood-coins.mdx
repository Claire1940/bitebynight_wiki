## Best Roles for Farming Scrap

Not all survivors are created equal when it comes to the economy. While the "Customer" is free and reliable, other roles have built-in mechanics that generate extra currency.

### 1. The Medic (The Efficiency King)
The Medic is the first role you should buy (800 Scrap). Because you can heal yourself and others, you stay in the match longer. Longer survival times equate to more XP and more opportunities to interact with objectives. Healing teammates is essentially "free" Scrap that the Customer role cannot access.

### 2. The Fighter (The Aggressive Earner)
Costing 1,350 Scrap, the Fighter is for players who are confident in their timing. By successfully parrying or stunning the animatronic with an axe swing, you earn "Stun" bonuses. This allows you to generate currency even while being chased, turning a dangerous situation into a profitable one.

### 3. The Security Guard (The Tactical Choice)
The Security Guard (1,100 Scrap) uses cameras to track the killer. While it doesn't have a direct "healing" or "stunning" bonus for Scrap, the ability to stay away from the killer means you can spend more uninterrupted time on generators, which is the most consistent way to earn.
