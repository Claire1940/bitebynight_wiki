## Understanding the "Rude" Hitbox System

One of the more technical aspects mentioned in the latest **Bite by Night Patch Notes** is the refinement of the "Rude" hitbox. In 2026, "Rude" refers to the server-sided hitbox of your character. Because there is always a slight delay between the client and the server, your "Rude" hitbox is where the killer actually sees you.

*   **Server-Side Tracking:** Your actual position is slightly behind your visual model during high-speed chases.
*   **Ping Dependency:** Players with higher ping will have a larger "Rude" offset, making them easier to hit around corners.
*   **Visual Aids:** You can enable hitbox visualization in the settings to see exactly where your server-side presence is located.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rely solely on your visual position when looping around doors. Always account for the "Rude" delay to avoid getting hit through closed objects.

## Advanced Survivor Techniques

With the changes to the Fighter's parry and the Security Guard's taser, high-level play now revolves around "stun-locking" the killer effectively. The Fighter can now build charges more reliably: one successful parry equals one swing. This allows for a defensive playstyle where you protect teammates while they focus on generators.

1.  **Door Tech:** Hold down the door interaction while running through to "clip" the killer's pathing, though this is harder to pull off after the recent 2026 physics update.
2.  **Taser Management:** As a Security Guard, don't waste your taser on a healthy killer. Save it for when a teammate is being carried or cornered.
3.  **Consumer Stamina:** Use the Bloxy Cola burst only when the killer is within striking distance. The following slowness effect makes you an easy target if you use it too early.

The **Bite by Night Patch Notes** also emphasize the importance of "Potato Mode" for players on lower-end hardware. Enabling this in the settings removes unnecessary particle effects, which can significantly improve your frame rate and reaction time during intense chases.
