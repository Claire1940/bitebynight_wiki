## Maximizing Performance and Settings

When playing on a **bite by night vip server**, you have the luxury of time to dive into the settings menu and optimize your game. Proper configuration can be the difference between successfully parrying a killer and being caught in a dead end.

### Essential Settings for 2026
*   **Camera Shake:** It is highly recommended to toggle this **OFF**. During intense chases, screen shaking can disorient you and cause you to miss crucial turns or barricade opportunities.
*   **Show Performance:** Enabling this allows you to monitor your Ping and FPS. Even in a private server, network spikes can occur, and knowing your latency helps with timing stuns.
*   **Potato Mode:** For players on mobile devices or older PCs, Potato Mode is essential. It lowers graphical fidelity to ensure a stable frame rate, which is vital for the fast-paced movement required in the Forest map.
*   **Center Stamina Bar:** This moves your stamina gauge from the corner of the screen to the center. Keeping your eyes on your stamina while kiting the killer is a pro-level habit that prevents "exhaustion deaths."

## Strategic Scrap Farming in Private Servers

Scrap is the lifeblood of progression in Bite By Night. While you can buy it with Robux, it is generally considered a poor investment because of how easy it is to earn through gameplay. Using a private server to farm scrap is a common tactic for players looking to unlock the more expensive killers quickly.

### The Farming Loop
1.  **Focus on Generators:** Each completed stage of a generator counts as a task. Completing all generators provides a massive Scrap bonus at the end of the round.
2.  **Battery Objectives:** Don't ignore the power boxes. Finding and installing batteries is a quick way to boost your score.
3.  **Disable Killer (Optional):** In some server configurations, you can effectively "farm" by having the player designated as the killer stay AFK or focus on environmental tasks, though active play usually yields better rewards per hour.
4.  **Survival Bonus:** Simply reaching the 6:00 AM escape mark provides a significant multiplier to your earned Scrap.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid buying the "2,500 Scrap for 500 Robux" pack in the shop. It is much more efficient to buy the VIP Game Pass once and earn your Scrap through boosted gameplay.

## Map Knowledge: Forest vs. Pizza Place

A **bite by night vip server** is the perfect environment to memorize map layouts. In 2026, there are three primary maps, each requiring a different strategy.

*   **The Forest:** This map is notoriously difficult for survivors due to its open nature and numerous dead ends. Practice using the "Fake Barricade" technique here—starting a barricade to bait the killer's swing, then immediately cancelling and running.
*   **The Pizza Place:** A more traditional indoor map with plenty of loops and doors. This is the best map for the Security Guard to utilize his camera system.
*   **The Warehouse:** A vertical map where height advantage is key. Use the elevators and stairs to break the killer's line of sight.
