This creates a more dynamic endgame, forcing the killer to patrol multiple escape routes simultaneously.

## Advanced Survival Strategies

With the buff to barricading, survivors now have a better chance of holding off killers in tight corridors. Barricades are harder for killers to break through, though the specific timing requires practice to master. 

1. **Prioritize Generators:** The objective "Repair generators to escape" is more vital than ever. At 6:00 a.m., any unrepaired generators will explode, signaling the final escape phase.
2. **Utilize the Fighter:** Because the Fighter now boasts 110 HP, they should be the primary "distractor" for the team, taking hits that would down a Medic or a standard survivor.
3. **Manage Scrap Wisely:** With the increased cost of survivors, don't spend all your scrap on emotes early on. Save for the Fighter or Security Guard to improve your win rate.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not rely solely on the 6:00 a.m. timer. The new update suggests that completing all generators may become a hard requirement for certain escape routes in future patches.
