## Essential Bite by Night Beginner Tips: Settings and Setup

Before you even step foot into a match, your first priority should be optimizing your game client. The default settings in Bite by Night are not always ideal for competitive play. High-level players often adjust these to gain better visibility and smoother movement.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **Camera Shake** | **OFF** | Prevents screen distortion during chases, making it easier to see the killer or survivor. |
| **Performance Mode** | **ON** | Displays your ping and server uptime; critical for timing parries and attacks. |
| **Potato Mode** | **ON (Low-end PCs)** | Boosts FPS significantly on mobile or older hardware by reducing graphical load. |
| **Hitboxes** | **ON** | Visualizes attack ranges for killers and fighters, ensuring your swings connect. |
| **Center Stamina Bar** | **ON** | Moves the stamina bar to the center of the screen so you don't lose focus during chases. |

> **💡 Tip:** Always play with your sound turned on. Audio cues are vital for hearing the killer’s footsteps or the hum of a nearby generator.

## Mastering the Survivor Role

As a survivor, your primary objective is to survive until 6:00 AM. You can accelerate this process by completing objectives like fixing generators and installing batteries. Every completed task reduces the time remaining, bringing you closer to the exit.

### Choosing Your Starter Class
Bite by Night features several roles, each with unique utility. As a beginner, your choice of class will dictate how much room for error you have during a match.

*   **Customer (Free):** The default role is surprisingly strong. The Pizza ability heals 20% HP, while the Drink provides a massive speed boost and temporary infinite stamina. It is excellent for learning how to loop killers.
*   **Medic:** Highly recommended for team players. You can heal yourself and teammates to full HP, which is invaluable in servers where the killer is aggressive.
*   **Fighter:** A skill-based role that allows you to fight back. You can stun the killer with an axe swing or parry their attacks to create an opening for escape.
*   **Security Guard:** The most expensive starter role, offering tactical advantages through cameras and a taser. You can highlight the killer's position for the entire team.

| Role | Primary Ability | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Customer** | Pizza & Energy Drink | Solo survival and escaping chases. |
| **Medic** | Self & Team Healing | Sustaining the team through long matches. |
| **Fighter** | Stun & Parry | Protecting teammates and aggressive play. |
| **Security Guard** | Taser & Cameras | Map awareness and scouting. |

### Generator and Battery Mechanics
To escape, you must interact with blue-wired panels to repair generators. This involves a mini-game where you must connect wires and rotate dials. To speed up the dial rotation, move your mouse in a circular motion rather than just up and down. Additionally, look for batteries scattered around the map. Taking these to power boxes is a quick way to progress the timer.

## Dominating as the Killer (Animatronic)

Playing the killer requires a different mindset. Instead of hiding, you must control the map and pressure survivors off objectives. The "Rotten" (Springtrap) is the standard starter killer and remains one of the most balanced options in 2026.

### Killer Abilities and Tactics
Most killers have a basic M1 swing, a secondary ranged or utility attack, and a trap or movement ability. For the Rotten, your bear traps are your best friend.

1.  **Bear Traps:** Place these at "choke points"—narrow hallways or doorways that survivors must pass through. A trapped survivor is usually a free kill.
2.  **Axe Throw:** Your Q ability throws an axe that deals bleed damage. Even if the survivor escapes the initial hit, their HP will slowly dwindle, making them easier to track.
3.  **Target Switching:** Do not get tunneled into chasing one skilled survivor for five minutes. If you see others repairing a generator, break off your chase and defend the objective.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Killers have a cooldown on their swings. Spamming left-click will leave you vulnerable to stuns and parries. Aim your shots carefully using Shift Lock.

## Map Strategy and Navigation

In 2026, Bite by Night features several distinct maps, each requiring a different approach. Memorizing the layout is the fastest way to improve your win rate.

*   **The Forest:** Widely considered the hardest map for survivors due to its open layout and lack of hiding spots. If you are a survivor here, focus on stuns and team coordination.
*   **The Pizza Place:** A classic indoor map with many rooms and doors. Use the "Fake Barricade" technique here—start a barricade to bait the killer into swinging, then immediately cancel and run through the door.
*   **The Storage Area:** Often where the final exit opens. As a killer, knowing exactly where the 6:00 AM exit is allows you to "gatekeep" the area during the final minute of the game.

## Farming Scrap Efficiently

Scrap is the lifeblood of your progression. You need it to unlock new roles like the Doppelganger or the Project. While you can buy scrap with Robux, it is generally considered a poor investment because earning it through gameplay is straightforward.

To maximize your scrap:
*   **Focus on Objectives:** Repairing generators and installing batteries yields significantly more scrap than simply hiding.
*   **Play Survivor:** Generally, survivors earn scrap faster through task completion than killers do through kills, unless the killer wipes the entire lobby quickly.
*   **VIP Pass:** If you enjoy the game, the VIP pass provides a permanent multiplier to your scrap earnings, making the grind much more manageable.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the best bite by night beginner tips for escaping the killer?**

A: The most effective strategy is stamina management. Never use more than 50% of your stamina unless you are actively being chased. If you are caught, use "looping" (running in circles around obstacles) and fake barricades to waste the killer's time while your teammates finish generators.

**Q: Which killer should I buy first with my scrap?**

A: While the Rotten is a great starter, many players recommend saving for the Doppelganger. Its ability to shapeshift into dead players can easily confuse inexperienced survivors, leading to easy kills. However, if you prefer a high-skill, high-reward playstyle, the Project offers multiple modes for strength and stealth.

**Q: How do I stop the survivors from fixing generators so fast?**

A: As a killer, you need to patrol the generators constantly. When you see progress on a generator, damage it or stay in the area to prevent them from returning. Using bear traps near active generators is also a highly effective deterrent.

**Q: Is the VIP Game Pass worth it in 2026?**

A: Yes, if you plan on playing regularly. It increases your scrap gain and provides exclusive cosmetics. Since scrap is used to unlock all gameplay-impacting roles, the VIP pass significantly speeds up your access to the full roster of characters.
