## Getting Started: The Core Objective

Bite by Night is an asymmetrical survival horror game that pits a team of survivors against a single, powerful killer. The primary goal for survivors is to stay alive until the clock hits 6:00 AM. While the timer ticks down naturally, survivors can significantly accelerate the process by completing map objectives. Once the timer reaches its limit, exit gates open, and survivors must reach them to win the round.

For the killer, the objective is simple: eliminate all survivors before they can escape. Each killer has a unique set of tools to track, trap, and down survivors, making the game a constant high-stakes game of cat and mouse.

## Survivor Classes and Abilities

When learning **Bite by Night how to play**, your first major decision is choosing a class. Each class brings a specific utility to the team, ranging from combat and stuns to healing and reconnaissance. As of the 2026 release, there are four primary classes available.

### The Customer (Starter Class)
The Customer is the default class and serves as a balanced "survivalist" template. While they lack specialized tools like tasers or axes, their self-sustain makes them great for beginners.
*   **Pizza (Q):** Heals the player for 20% of their current HP. This is vital for surviving a close encounter.
*   **Drink (E):** Grants a temporary speed boost to outrun the killer. Note that a slowness debuff applies briefly once the effect ends.

### The Fighter
The Fighter is the primary aggressive class, capable of direct confrontation.
*   **Swing (Q):** Lunges at the killer with an axe to apply a 3.5 to 5-second stun. 
*   **Parry (E):** A high-skill move that blocks an incoming attack. A successful parry stuns the killer for 1.5 seconds and grants "Charge" stacks to enhance your Swing ability.

### The Healer
The Healer is the backbone of team survival, though they are often targeted first by smart killers.
*   **Heal (Q):** Regenerates a teammate's health through a dedicated animation.
*   **Self Heal (E):** Allows the Healer to regenerate their own HP (on a 60-second cooldown).

### The Security Guard
Widely considered the best class in the current meta, the Security Guard provides unparalleled utility.
*   **Taser (Q):** A chargeable projectile that slows the killer. The longer you charge it, the more effective the stun.
*   **Cams (E):** Allows you to view the map through security cameras. You can "ping" the killer or generators to reveal their locations to the entire team.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing solo, the Security Guard's camera ability is the best way to coordinate with your team without using voice chat.

## Mastering the Killer Roster

Playing as the killer requires a different mindset. You must manage your "Malice" and utilize your powers to shut down loops and prevent generator completion.

### Springtrap (Trapper/Rushdown)
Springtrap is a hybrid killer who excels at area denial and sudden bursts of speed.
*   **Remnant Cleaver:** Throws an axe that applies a 15-second bleed effect to survivors.
*   **Bear Trap:** Places up to three traps that stun survivors for 10 seconds.
*   **Scream:** Reveals all survivor locations, regenerates 25 stamina, and blinds nearby enemies.
*   **Charge:** A high-speed dash that allows you to grab a survivor for massive damage.

### The Mimic (Stance Changer)
The Mimic is a highly adaptable killer with three distinct modes:
1.  **Strength Mode:** High damage, default stats.
2.  **Speed Mode:** 15% speed boost but 75% less damage.
3.  **Stealth Mode:** Becomes undetectable at the cost of 25% damage.

The Mimic can also use **Drill** to submerge into the floor, becoming invisible and dealing damage upon surfacing under a survivor.

### Ennard (Deception)
Ennard features a unique disguise mechanic, allowing him to take the form of survivors to lure them into a false sense of security before striking.

## Essential Mechanics: Generators and Barricades

To truly understand **Bite by Night how to play**, you must master the two most frequent minigames: repairing generators and holding doors.

### How to Repair Generators
Generators in Bite by Night are more complex than in other asymmetrical games. They require a three-step process:
1.  **Color Match:** Align the colored halves on the UI.
2.  **Light Grid:** Ensure all lights in the secondary tab are switched on.
3.  **The Tug:** Click and drag your cursor up and down to "pull" the lever until the segment is complete.

Successfully completing a generator takes a massive chunk of time off the 6:00 AM countdown, making them the highest priority for survivors.

### The Barricade Minigame
When a killer chases you into a room, you can interact with doors to barricade them. This triggers a "Center the Mouse" minigame similar to the mechanics found in games like *Pressure*.
*   You must keep your cursor inside a moving circle in the center of the screen.
*   If you succeed, the door stays shut, and your stamina regenerates while you hold it.
*   If you fail, the door is destroyed, you take knockback damage, and the killer gains an opening.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not hold a barricade until the door breaks. Use it to regain stamina, then bail before the killer smashes through to maintain your lead.

## Advanced Strategies for 2026

1.  **Loop Awareness:** Maps are filled with "loops" (areas where you can run in circles around obstacles). Use your stamina sparingly; only sprint when the killer is actively gaining on you.
2.  **The Scraps Economy:** Scraps are the primary currency in Bite by Night. You earn them by playing matches and completing objectives. Save your scraps for the **Security Guard** or **The Mimic**, as these characters offer the highest win rates in the current [official Bite by Night class index](https://www.gamezebo.com/walkthroughs/bite-by-night-classes-index/).
3.  **Killer Tracking:** As a survivor, listen for the unique chase music of each killer. Springtrap’s heavy mechanical footsteps are distinct from The Mimic’s skittering sounds.
4.  **Exit Strategy:** When the timer hits 6:00 AM, don't just run blindly. Use the Security Guard's cameras or a teammate's ping to locate the specific exit gate that has opened.

## FAQ

**Q: When is the official release date for Bite by Night?**

A: The game is scheduled for public release on March 28, 2026. Early access was previously available to select content creators and testers.

**Q: What is the best class for a beginner learning Bite by Night how to play?**

A: The Customer is excellent for beginners because of the straightforward "Pizza" healing ability. However, the Security Guard is widely considered the strongest class overall due to its ability to reveal the killer's location to the whole team.

**Q: How do I earn Scraps to unlock new characters?**

A: Scraps are earned by playing matches, completing generator objectives, and successfully escaping. You can also occasionally receive Scraps through promotional codes released by the developers.

**Q: Can the killer destroy the barricaded doors permanently?**

A: Yes. Once a killer wins the barricade minigame or hits a door enough times, it is destroyed and cannot be used again for the remainder of the round. Survivors should use doors strategically rather than relying on a single room for protection.
