## Phase 2: The Aggressive Transition

When the Rust Trap is without his axe—either because it was thrown or because of specific game triggers—his ability set shifts from defensive zoning to aggressive predation. This is often referred to as "Phase 2."

### The Scream and Charge Combo
In this state, your Bear Trap ability is replaced by **Scream** and **Charge**.
1.  **The Scream:** This ability provides a massive burst of speed and instantly refills your stamina bar. Expert players use "Scream-canceling" by waiting until their stamina is at 5% before activating the ability, effectively doubling their chase duration.
2.  **The Charge:** The Rust Trap lunges forward to grab a survivor. If successful, it triggers a short cutscene jumpscare, deals 40% of their current health, and leaves them ragdolled for 2 seconds.

### Stamina Management in Phase 2
While in Phase 2, your stamina drains faster during normal sprints. You must be decisive. If you cannot land a Charge within the first few seconds of a chase, it is often better to retreat and find your axe to reset your pressure.
