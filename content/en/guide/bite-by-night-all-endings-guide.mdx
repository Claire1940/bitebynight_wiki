## The Three Primary Ending Paths

In *Bite by Night*, "endings" are categorized by the survivor's method of departure or the Killer's success in preventing it. While most matches end in a standard victory or defeat, the 2026 update introduced nuanced variations that trigger unique cutscenes and lobby badges.

| Ending Type | Requirement | Reward/Outcome | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Escape** | Power 5 terminals and open the Exit Gates. | Standard XP and "Survivor" Badge. | Medium |
| **The Hatch Escape** | Be the Last Man Standing and find the hidden trapdoor. | Bonus XP and "Lone Wolf" Achievement. | Hard |
| **The Lore Cinematic** | Collect all Lore Notes and escape through the Hatch. | Unique Cinematic & "Historian" Title. | Very Hard |
| **Total Domination** | Killer eliminates all 4 survivors before any escape. | Max Killer XP & "Merciless" Badge. | Variable |

### 1. The Standard Exit Gate Ending
This is the most common conclusion to a match. Survivors must work together to repair terminal stations scattered across the map. Once the final terminal is powered, the exit gates receive power. 

To secure this ending, the **Engineer** class is highly recommended due to their 15% repair speed boost. However, the end-game collapse is dangerous. Killers like **Ennard** can use their Scooper Lunge to guard gates from a distance, while **Springtrap** can place Phantasms to alert him to gate interactions.

### 2. The Last Man Standing (Hatch Ending)
When three survivors have been eliminated or have escaped, the "Last Man Standing" mechanic activates. For the final player, a secret escape hatch opens at a random location on the map. This ending provides a massive boost to individual score but requires intimate map knowledge.

> **Warning:** The Killer can also see the hatch once it opens. If the Killer reaches it first, they can "stomp" it shut, forcing the survivor to attempt the exit gates instead.

## The Secret Lore Ending: Step-by-Step Guide

The most coveted conclusion in the 2026 update is the **Lore Cinematic Ending**. This isn't just a win; it’s a narrative revelation that connects the game to the broader FNaF lore. This **Bite by Night all endings guide** highlights the specific steps needed to trigger this rare sequence.

### Step 1: Collect the Lore Notes
Lore Notes are small, glowing papers hidden in randomized locations. However, they only spawn in specific high-traffic zones. You must collect all four notes in a single match.

*   **Note 1:** Usually found in the **Employee Lounge** or behind the **Gas Station** dumpster.
*   **Note 2:** Located near the **Boiler Room** red pipes or under the **Main Pizzeria** stage.
*   **Note 3:** Tucked inside a locker in the **Warehouse** upper floors.
*   **Note 4:** Found at the map boundary near the **Invisible Mime Wall** (Forest Map).

### Step 2: Activate the Secret Hatch
Unlike the standard hatch escape, the Lore Ending *requires* the hatch to be used even if other survivors are still alive. To force the hatch to open early, the team must have collected all Lore Notes and then interacted with a "Hidden Terminal" usually located in the basement or sub-levels of the map.

### Step 3: The Cinematic Reveal
Once the player carrying the notes jumps through the hatch, the match ends immediately for all players, and a unique cinematic plays. This sequence reveals the origin of the "Monster Rat" and hints at future DLC Killers coming later in 2026.

## Killer Strategies to Prevent Survivor Endings

If you are playing as the Killer, your goal is to deny these endings. Understanding how survivors achieve them is the first step to stopping them.

*   **Denying the Lore Ending:** Keep a close eye on the "Lore Note" spawn points. If you see a survivor lingering in the Boiler Room or the Employee Lounge without a terminal nearby, they are likely hunting for notes.
*   **Gate Guarding:** Use **Springtrap's Phantasms** to create a perimeter around the exit gates. If a survivor touches an illusion, their aura is revealed, allowing you to intercept them before they finish the opening animation.
*   **Hatch Control:** As soon as the second-to-last survivor is downed, start patrolling the map for the hatch sound (a low, wind-like whistling). Closing the hatch is the most effective way to ensure a "Total Domination" ending.

> **Expert Tip:** As **Ennard**, you can "detach" parts of your body to monitor the hatch while you chase the final survivor at the exit gates. This dual-pressure strategy is essential for high-rank play in 2026.

## Class Synergy for Ending Success

Achieving the more difficult endings, like the Lore Cinematic, is nearly impossible without a coordinated team. Each class plays a role in the **Bite by Night all endings guide** strategy.

1.  **The Scout:** Their ability to track the Killer's aura within 20m is vital for note-hunting. The Scout should be the one carrying the Lore Notes, as they have the best chance of avoiding the Killer while moving toward the hatch.
2.  **The Tank:** The Tank's role is to "loop" the Killer. By distracting the animatronic at "jungle gyms" or pallet loops, the Tank buys time for the Scout to find the hidden collectibles.
3.  **The Engineer:** While the others hunt for secrets, the Engineer must keep enough terminals powered to ensure the "Hidden Terminal" becomes interactable.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Lore Ending in a solo match?**

A: It is possible but extremely difficult. Since you have to collect all notes and find the hatch alone, using the **Scout** class is mandatory for the aura-reading capabilities. Without a team to distract the Killer, you will likely be caught before finding the third note.

**Q: Does the "Pied Piper" badge help with the Bite by Night all endings guide completion?**

A: Yes. While the Pied Piper badge doesn't trigger a new cinematic, it is a prerequisite for the "Ultimate Survivor" achievement, which many players consider the "True Completion" ending of the game.

**Q: What happens if the Killer closes the hatch while I have the Lore Notes?**

A: If the hatch is closed, you must attempt to open the exit gates. If you escape through the gates with the Lore Notes, you will still receive the "Historian" title, but the unique cinematic sequence will not play. The hatch is the only way to trigger the cutscene.

**Q: Are there any endings specific to the "Monster Rat" Killer?**

A: If the Monster Rat achieves a "Total Domination" win, a special execution animation plays for the final survivor, referencing his origins as a living creature rather than a standard animatronic. This is a unique "Killer Ending" added in the March 2026 update.
