## Core Bite by Night Objectives Explained

To win a match as a survivor, your team must complete a set of randomized tasks before the clock hits 6:00 AM. While the specific locations change based on the map, the fundamental **bite by night objectives** usually revolve around restoring power to the facility.

### Primary Task List

| Objective Type | Quantity Required | Primary Interaction | Scrap Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fix Generators** | 3 Generators | Wire dragging & Dial turning | High |
| **Install Batteries** | 2 Batteries | Finding & Carrying to Power Box | Medium |
| **Survive to 6 AM** | N/A | Stealth and Evasion | Completion Bonus |

> **💡 Tip:** Completing individual stages of a generator counts as a "task." Even if you don't finish the entire machine, you still earn scrap for the progress you made.

### How to Fix Generators
Generators are the most time-consuming part of the **bite by night objectives**. Each generator requires four successful mini-game completions. The mini-game involves:
1.  **Connecting Wires:** Dragging a colored wire to its matching port.
2.  **Turning Dials:** Rotating a dial until the indicator turns red.
3.  **Circular Motion:** Moving your cursor or finger in a circular pattern to speed up the process.

Always keep your camera positioned toward the nearest doorway while working. If you hear the killer's heartbeat or see a visual cue, abandon the generator immediately.

### Battery Mechanics
Batteries are scattered randomly across the map. Once you find one, you must carry it to a designated power box. 
*   **Pick up:** Use the interact key (E on PC).
*   **Drop:** Press X (on PC) to drop the battery if you need to run or use a flashlight.
*   **Installation:** Hold the interact key at the power box to slot the battery in.

*   **Customer:** The most balanced starter. The "Drink" ability provides a 3-second speed boost followed by 10 seconds of infinite stamina. This is perfect for escaping a killer when you are caught out in the open.

*   **Security Guard:** Excellent for coordinated teams. You can use cameras to ping the killer's location, allowing your teammates to work on generators on the opposite side of the map safely.

## Killer Overview and Counter-Play

While survivors focus on the **bite by night objectives**, the killer's goal is to eliminate all players before 6:00 AM. In 2026, there are three primary killers to watch out for.

| Killer | Difficulty | Key Ability | How to Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Rotted** | Easy | Bear Traps & Throwing Axe | Watch the floor for traps; dodge horizontally. |
| **The Project** | Hard | Three Modes (Speed, Stealth, Strength) | Listen for mechanical whirring; keep distance. |
| **Doppler Ganger**| Medium | Skin Stealer (Shapeshift) | Watch for "teammates" acting suspicious or not doing tasks. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The Rotted can place up to three bear traps at once. They are frequently placed in narrow hallways or right in front of active generators.

### The Last Man Standing Mechanic
If the killer eliminates everyone except for one survivor, the game timer automatically jumps to 5:00 AM. This gives the final survivor a fighting chance to escape. At 6:00 AM, the escape doors will open, and a visual marker will appear on your HUD.

## Map Strategies: Forest vs. Pizza Place

The layout of the map significantly impacts how easily you can complete the **bite by night objectives**.

### The Forest
This is widely considered the most difficult map for survivors. It is highly open with very few places to hide.
*   **Strategy:** Don't run in straight lines. Use the few trees and rocks available to break the killer's line of sight.
*   **Stun Usage:** Since there are fewer doors to barricade, the Fighter and Security Guard are essential on this map to keep the killer at bay.

### The Pizza Place (Default)
This map is much more indoor-focused, offering plenty of doors and tight corners for "jiking" the killer.
*   **Barricading:** You can barricade doors to slow the killer down. 
*   **Fake Barricades:** A pro tactic is to start the barricade animation and then immediately cancel it and run through. This often baits the killer into swinging early, giving you more time to escape.

## Optimized Settings for Better Performance

Before diving into your next match to complete the **bite by night objectives**, ensure your settings are optimized for competitive play.

1.  **Camera Shake:** Turn this **OFF**. It makes it much harder to navigate during high-intensity chases.
2.  **Center Stamina Bar:** Turn this **ON**. Having your stamina visible in the center of the screen allows you to manage your resources without looking away from the killer.
3.  **Hitboxes:** Turn these **ON**. This is particularly useful for Fighters and Killers to see exactly where an attack will land.
4.  **Potato Mode:** Enable this if you are playing on a mobile device or a low-end PC to increase your FPS and reduce input lag during generator mini-games.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the main bite by night objectives for survivors?**

A: Survivors must fix three generators and install two batteries into power boxes. Once these are complete and the time reaches 6:00 AM, players must reach the escape door to win.

**Q: How do I earn more scrap in Bite by Night?**

A: You earn scrap by completing tasks, such as fixing generator segments or successfully installing batteries. Survivors generally earn more scrap than killers, especially if they stay active throughout the match.

**Q: Can I buy the VIP Game Pass with in-game currency?**

A: Yes, in 2026, players have the option to purchase the VIP Game Pass using Scrap, though it is also available for 300 Robux. VIP provides significant stat multipliers and bonuses.

**Q: What is the best way to escape a chase in the Forest map?**

A: Since the Forest is open, your best bet is to use stamina-boosting items like the Customer's "Drink" or rely on a teammate to stun the killer with a taser or axe. Avoid running into dead ends, which are common on the map's borders.
