## FAQ

**Q: What is the main cause of the Bite by Night drama?**

A: Most of the drama stems from the high community expectations and the lack of a fixed release date. Because the game looks significantly better than most Roblox titles, there is constant speculation about whether it can actually run on the platform or if it will be delayed indefinitely.

**Q: Is Bite by Night an official Five Nights at Freddy's game?**

A: No, it is a fan-game created by OzelBlox and BlueRev. While it features characters from the FNaF franchise, it is an independent project and is not officially affiliated with Scott Cawthon or Fazbear Fanverse, though it follows the general spirit of FNaF fan-game creation.

**Q: Can I play Bite by Night right now?**

A: As of March 2026, the game is in the "soon to be released" phase. There are no public builds currently available to the general population, though private testing may be occurring with staff and select community members.

**Q: Which animatronics are confirmed for the game?**

A: Springtrap and Ennard are among the most prominently featured and confirmed killers. Monster Rat is currently listed as an "unconfirmed" killer on the wiki, though he remains a popular topic of discussion within the **Bite by Night drama** circles.
