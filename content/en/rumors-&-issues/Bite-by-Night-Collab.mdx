## Gameplay Mechanics and Classes

A **Bite by Night Collab** isn't just about the killers; it also involves how survivors interact with the world. The game features distinct "Classes" for survivors, allowing for specialized roles during a match. This is a departure from simpler Roblox horror games, leaning more into the tactical depth found in titles like *Dead by Daylight*.

### Survivor Objectives and Mechanics

Survivors must work together to complete specific objectives to escape. The "Last Man Standing" mechanic ensures that even if a team is dismantled, the final survivor has a narrow window of opportunity to make a dramatic exit. 

1.  **Objective Completion:** Survivors must interact with environmental puzzles and machinery to power the exit gates.
2.  **Stealth and Emotes:** Using the environment to hide is crucial. The game also features a variety of emotes available in the store to facilitate non-verbal communication.
3.  **Class Roles:** Each survivor can choose a class that provides buffs to repair speed, healing, or distraction capabilities.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not mistake **Monster Rat** for a standard animatronic. In the game's lore, he is a living creature, which may result in different AI behaviors or detection methods compared to the mechanical killers.
