## Tips for New Players

If you are just starting your journey in Bite by Night, the learning curve can be steep. Follow these expert tips to improve your survival rate:

*   **Listen Closely**: Sound design is a core mechanic. Killers often have distinct audio cues (clanking metal, heavy breathing) that reveal their location before you see them.
*   **Don't Run Constantly**: Running leaves scratch marks that killers can follow. Use walking and crouching to stay stealthy when the killer is nearby.
*   **Work Together**: Objectives are completed significantly faster when multiple technicians work on them simultaneously.
*   **Know Your Killer**: Each animatronic has a different "Terror Radius." If you are facing Ennard, be prepared for different tactics than if you were facing Springtrap.
