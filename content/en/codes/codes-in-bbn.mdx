## Troubleshooting Redemption Issues

Even with the correct **codes in bbn**, you might encounter technical glitches. If a code that is confirmed to be active isn't working for you, consider the following:

1. **Server Lag**: During major updates like the April 2026 QOL patch, servers can become congested. If your reward doesn't appear immediately, wait a few minutes or rejoin a different server.

3. **Previous Redemption**: Most codes are "one-time use" per account. If you have redeemed it in the past, you cannot claim the rewards again.
