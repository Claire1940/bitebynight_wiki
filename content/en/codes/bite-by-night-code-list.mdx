### 1. The Barricade Method
Barricading doors is no longer just a defensive move; it is a primary source of bonus Scrap. When a killer is actively chasing you, slamming a barricade in their path grants a "Stun" or "Block" bonus. 
*   **Risk vs. Reward:** Only attempt this if you have high health. If you are at half HP, the animation lock for barricading might allow the killer to land a finishing blow.
*   **Efficiency:** Each successful barricade that stops a killer's momentum provides a significant boost to your end-game total.

### 2. Battery Objectives
While generators are the standard objective, **Batteries** are the secret to high scores. Locating and installing batteries provides a higher flat bonus than simply repairing generators. Additionally, completing all tasks causes the extraction timer to speed up, ensuring you spend less time in danger and more time in the lobby spending your earnings.

### 3. Playing the Medic Role
If you play as the Medic class, you earn bonus Scrap for every health point you restore to your teammates. In a long match where the killer is aggressive but not getting kills, a Medic can rack up hundreds of extra Scrap just by acting as the team's mobile heal station.

### 4. Killer Maximization
When you are selected as the killer, your goal should be to end the round as quickly as possible. While survivors generally have a higher potential for Scrap through various tasks, a "Total Wipeout" as a killer yields a solid flat reward (around 600 Scrap) in a fraction of the time a full survivor round takes.

## Survival Tips for New Players

*   **Don't Camp Generators:** Killers often patrol a circuit of the three closest generators. If you see the killer approaching, move to a generator on the opposite side of the map rather than hiding in a nearby locker.
*   **Use Audio Cues:** The game features directional audio. If you hear mechanical whirring or heavy footsteps, the killer is likely within 30 studs of your position.
*   **Parry Timing:** If you are playing the Fighter class, wait until the killer is mid-swing to use your stun ability. Using it too early results in a miss and a long cooldown, leaving you vulnerable.
