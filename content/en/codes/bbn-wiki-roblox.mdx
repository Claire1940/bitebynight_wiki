## Scrap Farming and the Economy

Scrap is the lifeblood of your progression in Bite by Night. While the temptation to purchase scrap with Robux exists, experienced players strongly advise against it. As of 2026, the game has balanced the economy to favor active gameplay. The daily scrap cap currently sits at 525, a reduction from the previous 1,000, but this is offset by significantly buffed earnings per match. To maximize your efficiency, you should focus on playing as a Survivor.

| Method | Scrap Yield | Efficiency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solo Generator Repair** | High | Best for individual growth |
| **Team Repair** | Medium | Faster completion, lower payout |
| **Killer Victories** | Variable | High risk against pro teams |
| **VIP Pass Bonus** | **+20%** | Mandatory for serious grinders |

The most effective way to farm is to disable the "Killer" option in your settings. This ensures you always spawn as a Survivor, which generally offers more consistent scrap rewards. When the match begins, aim to complete at least two generators entirely on your own. Repairing a generator solo grants the full currency reward, whereas sharing the task splits the payout among all participants.

## Survivor Roles and Tier List

Choosing the right role can drastically change your survival rate. Each survivor has a specific niche, but in the current 2026 meta, some roles clearly outshine others in terms of utility and scrap generation.

### Survivor Comparison Table

| Role | Primary Ability | Best For | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Security Guard** | Camera System & Taser | Intel & Scrap Farming | Easy |
| **Fighter** | Combat Stuns | Protecting Teammates | Hard |
| **Medic** | Healing | Team Sustainability | Medium |
| **Customer** | Basic Survival | Learning Maps | Easy |

The **Security Guard** is arguably the strongest role for those looking to master the **bbn wiki roblox** mechanics. By using your tablet, you can highlight the killer’s location for the entire team. This not only keeps you safe but provides vital information that can win the game. However, be mindful of your tablet's battery; do not spam the cameras, or you will find yourself defenseless when the killer finally tracks you down.

The **Medic** role features a unique mechanic that often confuses new players. While you can cancel the healing animation when assisting a teammate, you are locked into the animation when healing yourself. This makes self-healing extremely risky if the killer is nearby.

## Killer Mechanics and Counters

Playing as the killer requires a different mindset. You are not just a brute-force hunter; you are a tactician. The **Mimic** (also known as the Project) remains the top-tier killer in 2026 due to its high base damage of 40 and its ability to traverse the map by digging underground.

### Killer Stats Overview

| Killer | Base Damage | Unique Mechanic | Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mimic (Project)** | **40** | Digging/Mode Swap | S |
| **Enard (Doppelganger)** | 30 | Skin Stealing/Blind | A |
| **Springtrap** | 35 | Free/Balanced | B |

To counter **Enard**, you must pay close attention to the survivor icons in the bottom-left corner of your UI. Enard can steal the skin of a fallen player to disguise itself. If you see a "player" approaching you, but their icon in the UI has a red cross over it, you are looking at the killer. If Enard manages to blind you, quickly shake your camera/mouse to clear the effect.

## Competitive Settings and Performance

Bite by Night is a high-stakes game where a single frame drop can result in your demise. This is especially true on the **Forest Map**, which is currently the most resource-intensive area in the game. If you experience lag, switching to "Potato Mode" in the settings is highly recommended.

### Recommended Settings for 2026

1.  **Disable Camera Shake:** This prevents your screen from wobbling during chases, allowing for better navigation.
2.  **Enable Hitboxes:** This provides a visual representation of where your attacks (or the killer's) will land.
3.  **Center Stamina Bar:** Keeps your resource management directly in your line of sight.
4.  **Flashlight Off at Spawn:** A flashlight makes you an easy target in the dark. Only use it when absolutely necessary.
5.  **Show Barriers:** Highlights invisible walls in white, preventing you from getting stuck in a corner during a pursuit.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to farm scrap in private servers. The developers have disabled all currency and XP gains in private sessions to prevent exploitation.

## Map Strategy: The Final Hour

As the clock approaches 5:00 AM, the atmosphere in the pizzeria shifts. The music intensifies, and the pressure to find the exit increases. You must reach the escape point before 6:00 AM, or the building will begin to burn, resulting in an automatic death for anyone left inside.

Memorizing the escape points for each map (Forest, Pizzeria, etc.) is the difference between a successful run and a total loss. In the final hour, stop focusing on generators and move toward the known exit zones. If you are playing as the Security Guard, use your cameras one last time to ensure the path to the exit is clear of the killer.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I stop the blindness effect from Enard?**

A: When Enard blinds you, a "shake" mechanic is triggered. You must rapidly move your mouse or shake your screen to clear the darkness and regain your vision.

**Q: Is the VIP pass worth the 15,000 scrap?**
