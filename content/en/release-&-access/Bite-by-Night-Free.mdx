## How to Spot and Avoid Roblox Scams
Scammers have become increasingly sophisticated in 2026. They use several psychological tactics to trick players into spending Robux. One common method involves creating a "lobby" that looks legitimate but contains "double-gate" game passes. 

For example, a player might pay 25 Robux to enter the game, only to find a lobby that requires *another* 100 Robux game pass to "unlock the real map." In many cases, these games are scripted to keep players rejoining automatically. This "rejoin loop" inflates the game's "Active Players" and "Total Visits" metrics, making it look popular and trustworthy to unsuspecting users.

### Common Red Flags to Watch For:
1. **The Rejoin Loop:** The game forces you to reconnect every few minutes to boost its ranking on the Discovery page.
2. **Infinite Obbies:** You are told that completing a difficult "obby" (obstacle course) will grant access to the game, but the finish line triggers no events.
3. **Fake Group Requirements:** Claims that joining a specific group will grant "Free Early Access," which is usually a ploy to grow a bot-filled group.
4. **Urgency Tactics:** On-screen prompts that tell you a "Limited Time Discount" on access is about to expire.
