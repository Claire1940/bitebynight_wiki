## Mastering the Bite by Night Console Layout

Playing an asymmetrical horror game on a controller requires precision and quick reflexes. The developers at OzelBlox and BlueRev have ensured that the input lag is minimized for the **Bite by Night Console** experience. Unlike PC players who rely on mouse flicks, console users benefit from analog movement, which can provide smoother navigation through tight corners in the pizzeria maps.

Below is the standard controller configuration for survivors and killers as of the 2026 launch.

| Action | Xbox Controller | PlayStation Controller | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Movement** | Left Thumbstick | Left Thumbstick | Standard 360-degree movement |
| **Camera Control** | Right Thumbstick | Right Thumbstick | Sensitivity can be adjusted in settings |
| **Interact/Objective** | **X** Button | **Square** Button | Used for repairing and healing |
| **Vault/Jump** | **A** Button | **Cross** Button | Essential for escaping killers |
| **Primary Ability** | **Right Trigger (RT)** | **R2** Button | Killer attacks or survivor items |
| **Secondary Ability** | **Left Trigger (LT)** | **L2** Button | Specific character perks/skills |
| **Crouch/Stealth** | **B** Button | **Circle** Button | Reduces noise and visibility |
| **Inventory/Emotes** | D-Pad | D-Pad | Accesses quick-use items |

> **💡 Tip:** Go into the "Settings" menu immediately upon your first login to adjust your "Camera Sensitivity." High sensitivity is recommended for Killers like Springtrap to keep track of survivors attempting to "360" or juke your attacks.

## Optimizing Performance on Console

To ensure the best **Bite by Night Console** experience, players should look into the Roblox engine settings. Because Bite by Night features high-quality models and complex lighting effects (especially for the animatronic skins), maintaining a stable frame rate is crucial for competitive play.

1.  **Manual Graphics Mode:** Set your graphics to "Manual" in the Roblox escape menu.
2.  **Level Selection:** For older console hardware (like the Xbox One or PS4), a graphics level of 4-6 is usually the "sweet spot" for 60 FPS.
3.  **UI Scaling:** Ensure the UI is scaled so that the "Skill Check" circles are easily visible on your television screen. Missing a skill check on console can be devastating during the late game.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Playing on "Automatic" graphics settings may cause sudden frame drops during intense chase sequences where multiple particle effects (like smoke or sparks) are present.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Bite by Night Console version different from the PC version?**

A: No, the game features full cross-play and cross-progression. The **Bite by Night Console** experience includes the same maps, killers, and mechanics as the PC version, but with a UI specifically optimized for controller navigation and television displays.

**Q: How do I trigger Springtrap's "I Smell Fear" ability on a controller?**

A: By default, special abilities are mapped to the **Left Trigger (LT)** or **L1** button. You must fill your "Hunt Meter" by staying in close proximity to survivors before the ability becomes active.

**Q: Can I play Bite by Night on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X?**

A: Yes, the game is fully compatible with next-gen hardware through the Roblox application. These consoles will offer the highest fidelity, including improved shadows and faster loading times for the pizzeria environments.

**Q: Will there be non-FNAF killers added in the future?**

A: According to the developers, the primary killers will remain FNAF-based to maintain the game's core identity. However, they have stated that crossover skins for existing killers may include non-FNAF content in future 2026 updates.
