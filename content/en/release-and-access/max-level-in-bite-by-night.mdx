## Mastering Hoax Springtrap at Max Level

Hoax Springtrap has quickly become a fan favorite due to his "Purple Guy" aesthetic and high-skill-cap mechanics. When you reach the **max level in bite by night** with this character, you aren't just playing a skin; you are wielding a precision tool for lobby control. His primary weapon, the throwing axe, is his most lethal asset, but it requires practice to use effectively.

To dominate like a pro, you must master the trajectory of your projectiles. A common mistake among newer players is aiming directly at the survivor's current position. Instead, follow these professional aiming tips:

*   **Aim Slightly Upward:** When throwing the axe, tilt your screen slightly higher than your target. This adjustment allows the axe to travel a greater distance and follow a straighter path, making it significantly harder for survivors to dodge.
*   **Predictive Pathing:** Don't throw where the survivor is; throw where they are going. At Level 20, you should be able to read survivor movement patterns near pallets and windows.
*   **Control the Pace:** You don't always need to rush. As a high-level killer, your presence alone creates pressure. Force survivors into "dead zones" where they have no cover.
