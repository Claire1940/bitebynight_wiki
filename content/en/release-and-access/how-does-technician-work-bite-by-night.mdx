## How Does Technician Work Bite by Night: Abilities Explained

The core of the Technician's gameplay revolves around two primary abilities: the **Bunny Mask** and the **Static Mirage**. These tools allow for both offensive area denial and defensive escape maneuvers.

### The Bunny Mask (Primary Ability)
Activated by pressing the **Q** key, the Bunny Mask is the Technician's signature tool. When you put the mask on, your perspective shifts into first-person mode, and your movement capabilities change drastically.

*   **Phasing:** While the mask is active, you can phase through specific structures and walls. This is vital for taking shortcuts that the killer cannot follow or escaping a "dead-end" room.
*   **The Dash (E):** While wearing the mask, you can press **E** to dash through highlighted obstacles. This is faster than normal phasing but comes with a steep cost.
*   **Cooldowns and Penalties:** The mask has a **30-second cooldown** once deactivated. Furthermore, using the Dash ability consumes **50% of your mask meter** and inflicts a **5-second slow debuff** on your character.

> **Warning:** Do not spam the Dash ability. The 5-second slow debuff makes you an easy target if the killer is right behind you and you haven't successfully broken line of sight.

### Static Mirage (Secondary Ability)
The Static Mirage is your defensive trap. This ability is only available when the Bunny Mask is **off**. By pressing **E**, you place a trap on the ground that remains active for **20 seconds**.

If a killer walks into the Static Mirage, they are hit with a **Slow** and **Daze** effect. This briefly impairs their vision and movement, giving you enough time to reach a new hiding spot or finish a generator. However, be aware that killers can see and destroy these traps if they are being observant.

## Strategic Gameplay and Movement

To truly understand **how does technician work bite by night**, you have to look past the buttons and focus on the "flow" of the match. The Technician is a master of repositioning. You should never be in the same spot for more than a few seconds after a killer spots you.

### Stamina Management
Since your stamina is a standard 100, you cannot afford to hold the shift key indefinitely. Expert Technicians toggle between walking and running to keep their meter above 50% at all times. If you are caught with zero stamina and your Bunny Mask is on cooldown, you are almost guaranteed to be captured.

### Trap Placement Tactics
Where you put your Static Mirage determines its effectiveness. Avoid placing traps in the middle of wide-open hallways where they are easily spotted.

1.  **Generator Protection:** Place a trap just around the corner of a generator you are working on. Killers often rush straight toward the sound of a repairing generator and won't look at the floor.
2.  **Doorway Blocking:** After closing or blocking a door, place a trap immediately on the other side. When the killer breaks the door and lunges through, they will trigger the daze effect.
3.  **Escape Paths:** If you are being chased, drop a trap in a narrow "choke point" like a staircase or a small doorway.

|---|---|---|
| **Behind Doors** | High | Killers have limited visibility when entering rooms. |
| **Near Generators** | High | Killers are distracted by the objective. |
| **Open Fields** | Low | Traps are highly visible and easily destroyed. |

## Advanced Tips for 2026 Meta

As the game evolves on the [official Roblox platform](https://www.roblox.com/games/), the strategies for survivors must also adapt. In the current 2026 meta, the Technician is often used as a "distraction" or "kiter."

*   **Breaking Line of Sight:** The Bunny Mask is best used when you are already behind a wall. If the killer sees you phase, they know exactly where you went. If you phase through a wall while they are momentarily blinded or looking away, they will lose your trail entirely.
*   **The "Mask-Off" Transition:** Remember that you cannot place traps while the mask is on. A common mistake is trying to trap a killer during a chase while still in first-person mode. You must press **Q** to remove the mask before you can use your **E** trap.
*   **Forcing Bad Situations:** Use your phasing to lead the killer into areas of the map that are difficult for them to navigate, then phase through a wall to leave them stuck in a long animation or a dead end.

## Synergy with Other Survivors
The Technician works best when paired with survivors who can provide information or healing. While you are busy trapping generators, having a "Scout" or "Medic" nearby ensures that if you do take a hit during your 5-second dash debuff, you can be brought back into the fight quickly.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** If you are playing with a coordinated team, call out when your Bunny Mask is on cooldown. This tells your teammates that you are currently vulnerable and might need them to pull the killer's aggro.

## FAQ
