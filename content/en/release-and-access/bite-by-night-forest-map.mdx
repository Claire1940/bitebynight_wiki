In the forest, sound travels differently. Footsteps on dry leaves are louder than on damp moss. Players should pay close attention to the ground texture they are traversing. 

1. **Crouch-walking** on moss minimizes noise but reduces speed.
2. **Sprint-jumping** across creeks can prevent the "splashing" sound effect from triggering multiple times.
3. **Using the wind** to mask the sound of heavy breathing or gear rustling.
