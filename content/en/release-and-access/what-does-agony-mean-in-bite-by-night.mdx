## The Agony Mechanic Explained

The Agony system in Bite by Night acts as a "pity" or "queue" system. In many multiplayer horror games, the selection of the killer can feel random, leading to situations where one player might go hours without getting the role they want. Bite by Night solves this by tracking your "Agony."

### How Agony Works
Every time you play a match as a Survivor, your Agony level increases. This represents the "suffering" of the character, and eventually, that Agony boils over, turning you into the monster. Once you are finally selected to be the Killer, your Agony resets to zero (or a baseline level), and the cycle begins again.

| Feature | Impact on Agony | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Playing as Survivor** | Increases Agony | Higher chance to be Killer next round |
| **Playing as Killer** | Resets Agony | Lower chance to be Killer next round |
| **Purchasing VIP** | Multiplies Stats | Faster progression through levels |
| **Disabling Killer** | N/A | Removes you from the selection pool |

> **💡 Tip:** If you strictly want to farm Scrap and have no interest in being the Killer, you can go into your gameplay settings and toggle "Disable Killer." This prevents your Agony from putting you in the hunter role, allowing you to focus on survivor objectives which typically grant more Scrap.

## Character Roles: Killers and Survivors

Once you understand **what does agony mean in bite by night**, you need to know what to do when that meter hits its peak and you finally become the hunter. Conversely, you need to know which Survivor best suits your defensive style.

### The Killer Roster
The game currently features three distinct killers, each requiring a different skill level.

*   **The Rotted:** The starter killer. He uses a cleaver that can be thrown to track survivors and place bear traps to lock down loops.
*   **The Project:** A high-skill killer with three modes (Strength, Speed, Stealth). He can leap over objects and grab players, but requires significant practice to master.
*   **Doppler Ganger:** A fan favorite that costs 6,000 Scrap. He can shapeshift into other players to blend in and summon minions called "Wire Eyes" to reveal survivor locations.

### The Survivor Roster
Surviving until 6:00 AM requires a mix of stealth and utility.

*   **Customer:** The default survivor. He has a pizza for healing and a drink for a massive 3-second speed boost followed by 10 seconds of infinite stamina.
*   **Medic:** Focused on team utility, the Medic can heal others and themselves, making them essential for group survival.
*   **Fighter:** A combat-oriented survivor who can parry the killer's attacks and stun them with an axe.
*   **Security Guard:** The most expensive survivor (900 Scrap). He uses a taser to stun killers and can access cameras to ping the killer's location for the whole team.

## Survival Strategies for 2026

Surviving in Bite by Night is about more than just knowing **what does agony mean in bite by night**. You must master the maps and objective mechanics to earn enough Scrap for upgrades.

### Objective Management
The primary goal is to fix three generators and install two batteries.
*   **Generators:** These require a mini-game where you connect wires and rotate dials. Doing this in a circle is faster than clicking.
*   **Batteries:** These are scattered around the map. Pick them up and find the power boxes to install them.
*   **Scrap Farming:** You earn the most Scrap by completing tasks. If you are the last survivor alive, the clock automatically jumps to 5:00 AM, giving you a final chance to escape.

### Map-Specific Tips
There are currently three main maps: The Forest, The Pizza Place, and the Research Facility.
*   **The Forest:** This is widely considered the hardest map for survivors because it is wide open with many dead ends. If you are being chased here, your best bet is to use "Fake Barricades"—starting a barricade to bait the killer's swing, then immediately running through.
*   **The Pizza Place:** This map offers many doors and tight corners, making it significantly easier to "juke" the killer and break line-of-sight.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never let your stamina drop to zero while not in a chase. If the killer finds you while you are exhausted, you will have no way to reach a safe loop or barricade.

## Advanced Killer Tactics

When your Agony meter finally puts you in the Killer role, your objective is to eliminate survivors one by one. While some players find "targeting" controversial, it is objectively the most efficient way to win.

1.  **Trap Placement:** As The Rotted, place your bear traps in the middle of doorways or at the base of stairs. Survivors often look at the ceiling or behind them during a chase and will walk straight into them.
2.  **Axe Tracking:** The throwing axe isn't just for damage; it applies a bleed effect that allows you to see the survivor's outline through walls.
3.  **Baiting Parries:** If you are facing a Fighter, don't swing immediately. Walk up to them and wait for them to miss their parry, then strike while their ability is on cooldown.

## FAQ

**Q: What does agony mean in bite by night exactly?**

A: Agony is a value shown in the lobby that represents your probability of being chosen as the Killer in the next round. It increases as you play matches as a Survivor and resets once you play a round as the Killer.

**Q: Is it worth buying Scrap with Robux?**

A: Generally, no. The source material suggests that buying 2,500 Scrap for 500 Robux is not a good deal because Scrap is relatively easy to farm by simply completing generator objectives and surviving rounds.

**Q: How do I escape when the timer hits 6:00 AM?**

A: Once the clock reaches 6:00 AM, an escape door or elevator will open on the map. You must locate this exit and walk into it to successfully win the round and receive your survival bonus.

**Q: Can I see other players' Agony levels?**

A: Yes, in the intermission lobby, you can see the Agony levels of all players currently in the server. This helps you predict who might be the killer in the next game.
