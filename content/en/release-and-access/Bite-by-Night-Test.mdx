## Understanding the Bite by Night Test Mechanics

The core of the game revolves around a 4v1 asymmetrical format. One player takes on the role of a powerful Killer, while four others play as Survivors (or "Classes") attempting to complete objectives and escape the map. During the **Bite by Night Test**, the development team is specifically looking at how "Last Man Standing" mechanics affect the endgame pacing.

The game features a variety of interactive elements, including lockers for hiding, generators or similar objectives for progress, and unique character abilities. Unlike many other Roblox horror clones, Bite by Night emphasizes atmospheric dread and mechanical depth. The test environment is currently being used to fine-tune the "Terror Radius" and the efficiency of survivor classes.

| Feature | Description | Testing Priority |
|---|---|---|
| **Last Man Standing** | Buffs granted to the final survivor to allow for a clutch escape. | High |
| **Objective Speed** | The time required for survivors to complete tasks to open exits. | Medium |
| **Killer Hitboxes** | Ensuring accuracy in melee and ability-based attacks. | High |

## The Killer Roster: Ennard and Beyond

The Killers in Bite by Night are the stars of the show, featuring a mix of mainline FNaF characters and fan-made icons. During the current **Bite by Night Test**, Ennard has been a primary focus for balancing. ## Survivor Classes and Team Strategy

Survivors are not just generic avatars; they are divided into classes that define their role within the team. During the **Bite by Night Test**, players are encouraged to experiment with different class combinations to see which team compositions are most effective against the current Killer meta.

1.  **The Mechanic:** Specializes in completing objectives faster. Essential for high-speed escapes.
2.  **The Medic:** Capable of healing teammates quickly, reducing the downtime after a Killer encounter.
3.  **The Scout:** Has enhanced mobility and vision, making them perfect for tracking the Killer's position.
4.  **The Distractor:** Equipped with tools to stun or distract the Killer, buying time for the rest of the team.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Being a "Lone Wolf" is rarely successful in the **Bite by Night Test**. Most Killers are designed to punish isolated players, making teamwork the most viable path to survival.

## Maps and Environmental Objectives

The maps in Bite by Night are designed to be "hellish lands" where knowledge of the layout is as important as mechanical skill. From the lobby to the in-game arenas, every corner is filled with lore and tactical opportunities.

During the testing phase, the developers are monitoring "looping" spots—areas where survivors can lead the killer in circles to waste time. If a loop is found to be too strong, it is adjusted in real-time updates. The objectives themselves are varied, requiring survivors to interact with specific machines while managing their "Fear" levels.

### Objective Checklist for Survivors
*   **Locate Objectives:** Use the Scout's abilities to find the primary tasks.
*   **Maintain Stealth:** Avoid running constantly, as it leaves tracks for the Killer.
*   **Coordinate Saves:** If a teammate is captured, use a Distractor to facilitate a rescue.
*   **Power the Exits:** Once all objectives are complete, head to the escape gates immediately.
