## Strategies for Rapid Leveling in 2026

To reach the **bite by night max level** efficiently, you need to maximize your XP gain in every match. The game rewards performance metrics such as successful hits, hookings (for killers), and generator repairs or saves (for survivors).

## Advanced Combat Tips for Max Level Killers

Once you hit the **bite by night max level**, the game becomes about micro-decisions. For example, knowing when to "slug" (leave a survivor on the ground) versus when to hook them can determine the outcome of a match.

*   **Preventing Free Saves**: If you notice a specific survivor is playing a "medic" role, focus them down. Removing the player who provides the most utility to the survivor team makes the rest of the match significantly easier.
*   **Predictive Aiming**: Don't aim where the survivor is; aim where they are going to be. This is especially true for Springtrap's axe.
*   **Pressure Management**: Don't spend too long chasing a single survivor if three others are working on objectives. A max-level player knows when to break off a chase to apply pressure elsewhere.
