This passive ensures that the Technician is the primary choice for "gen-rushing" strategies. By cutting down the time spent on each puzzle, the survivor team can pressure the killer across multiple objectives simultaneously.

1.  **Locate the Generator:** Identify the closest objective at the start of the match.
2.  **Initiate Puzzle:** Open the interface to see the **Fixer Upper** perk in action.
3.  **Complete Remaining Wires:** Focus on the remaining connections to finish the objective 20-30% faster than other roles.

### The Audio Lure
The Audio Lure is a placeable gadget that can be attached to any flat surface. Once active, it emits sounds that mimic survivor activity, such as footsteps, drinking a Bloxy Cola, or the faint hum of a generator. 

*   **Cooldown:** 35 Seconds
*   **Effect:** If a killer enters the radius and investigates the sound source, they receive a temporary "Slowness" debuff.
*   **Counterplay:** Killers can destroy the lure if they locate the physical speaker (20 HP).

### The Illusion Disc
For more advanced players, the Illusion Disc provides a high-risk, high-reward distraction. When placed on the ground, it projects a hologram of the Technician. This hologram mimics basic survivor movement, wandering aimlessly until a killer enters its terror radius. At that point, the hologram will begin sprinting away, baiting the killer into a chase.

> **💡 Tip:** Place an Illusion Disc near a nearly-completed generator. The killer will often chase the hologram, giving you enough time to finish the objective and escape using your phasing ability.

To truly excel with the Technician, you must learn the "Prop-Hop." This technique involves baiting the killer toward a large piece of debris and phasing through it at the very last millisecond. Because the killer has to path around the object, you gain a massive lead.
