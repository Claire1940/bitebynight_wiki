## Strategies for the Snake Mini-Game
Since the high score is the mechanical gatekeeper for the skin, follow these tips to improve your Snake performance:

*   **Keyboard over Controller:** Use a mechanical keyboard for more precise "tap" movements. The latency on controllers can make tight turns impossible at high speeds.
*   **The "Border" Strategy:** Keep your snake primarily along the outer edges of the screen until you are forced to move inward for food. This keeps the center clear for emergency maneuvering.
*   **Shift Lock:** Some players report that toggling Shift Lock off during the arcade sequence helps maintain a consistent camera angle, preventing accidental deaths.
