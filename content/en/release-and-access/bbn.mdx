## Navigating the Impersonator Crisis

With the rising popularity of the game, bad actors have begun targeting the community. A massive TikTok account has recently been identified as an impersonator, gaining over 100,000 followers by posing as the official **bbn** social media presence. This account has been responsible for spreading fake teasers, including a fraudulent video for a "Puppet" character that was never authorized by the development team.

It is crucial to verify where you get your news. Fake teasers can lead to false expectations and can sometimes be used to distribute phishing links or scams.

1.  **Check the Handle:** Official accounts will often be linked directly from the [Bite By Night Roblox Page](https://www.roblox.com).
2.  **Verify via Discord:** The official Discord server is the most reliable source for real-time patch notes and announcements.
3.  **Look for Developer Interaction:** Genuine teasers are usually reposted or commented on by Ozel or other confirmed staff members.
4.  **Report Impersonators:** If you encounter an account spreading fake news, use the platform's reporting tools to protect other players.

> **💡 Tip:** Always cross-reference "leaks" found on TikTok with the official Discord announcements. If it isn't in the Discord, it's likely fake.
