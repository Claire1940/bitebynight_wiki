## Hitbox Overhauls and General Gameplay Changes

One of the most significant aspects of the recent update is the total rework of hitboxes. For a long time, players complained about "phantom hits" or tasers missing targets that appeared to be directly in front of them. The developers have addressed this by improving the registration for both melee attacks and projectiles.

The "Red Outline" system is another major addition. Killers can now see a faint red outline around survivors, but only when they are within a direct line of sight. This is intended to help killers track targets in the dark or through cluttered environments, though many players have noted that the current shade of red is somewhat brownish and difficult to see on lower graphics settings.

| Feature | Change Type | Impact Level | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hitbox Registration** | Optimization | **High** | Improved accuracy for killer swings and survivor parries. |
| **Red Outlines** | Visual Aid | **Medium** | Highlights survivors in direct line of sight for the killer. |
| **Dead Chat** | Restriction | **High** | Dead players can no longer communicate with living survivors. |
| **Kill Time Scaling** | Mechanic | **High** | Killers now gain more time per kill (20-25 seconds). |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing as a survivor, do not rely on dead teammates to scout for you in the chat. The removal of "ghost-calling" means you must rely entirely on your own senses and the in-game UI.

## Survivor Role Adjustments: The Great Nerf

The **bite by night upcoming updates** have been particularly heavy-handed toward the survivor roles. The goal appears to be moving away from "bully squads" that could indefinitely stun the killer. The Security Guard, formerly one of the most powerful roles in the game, has seen its utility gutted to prevent stun-chaining.

### Security Guard and Medic Changes
The Security Guard's taser now has a significantly longer cooldown. If you miss or cancel your taser, you are punished with a 15-second wait, while a successful shot now takes 20 seconds to recharge. Furthermore, the "Stun Immunity" mechanic ensures that a killer cannot be stunned by multiple survivors at the same time. If three guards try to tase a killer simultaneously, only the first one will register, and the killer will gain a brief window of immunity.

| Role | Attribute | Old Value | New Value |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Security Guard** | Taser Cooldown | 15 Seconds | **20 Seconds** |
| **Security Guard** | Cancel Cooldown | 10 Seconds | **15 Seconds** |
| **Medic** | Overheal Cap | +25 HP | **+15 HP** |
| **Customer** | Healing Speed | Normal | **Slowed Movement** |

The Medic has also seen a reduction in its protective capabilities. The overheal "shield" has been reduced by 40%, making survivors much more vulnerable to high-damage killers like the Anomaly. Additionally, the Customer role now experiences a significant movement speed penalty while eating pizza, preventing them from healing effectively during a chase.

## Killer Buffs and Ability Tweaks

While survivors are feeling the heat, killers have received several "Quality of Life" buffs that make them much more formidable. The Anomaly (Enard) is the big winner of this update, receiving both mechanical buffs and new cosmetic flair.

### Anomaly and Rotten Enhancements
The Anomaly's grab ability (E) is now fully aimable, allowing for much more precision when snatching survivors out of a sprint. Previously, the grab felt locked into a specific forward trajectory, but the new system allows for flick-shots. The Rotten can now pick up its own traps, allowing players to reposition their defenses if the survivors move to a different area of the map.

*   **Mimic:** The "Stealth Drill" radius has been increased from 10 to 13, a 25% increase in the area of effect, making it much harder for survivors to dodge the emergence.
*   **Mime:** New visual effects (VFX) have been added to the Q ability and the disguise transformation, making the character feel more polished.
*   **Enard:** Now has the ability to emote while disguised as a human, allowing for much more effective "social stealth" and trickery.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The new stun immunity means you should never stack your stuns. If a teammate stuns the killer, wait at least 3-5 seconds before attempting another stun, or your ability will be wasted.
