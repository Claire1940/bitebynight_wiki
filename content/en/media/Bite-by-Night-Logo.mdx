## Gameplay Mechanics: Survival in the Dark

Bite by Night is heavily inspired by the asymmetrical horror genre, where one player takes on the role of a powerful Killer while others play as Survivors (Classes). The game mechanics are designed to be intense, requiring coordination and a deep understanding of the map layout.

### Killer vs. Survivor Dynamics
The core of the game revolves around the "Last Man Standing" mechanic and objective-based gameplay. Survivors must complete specific tasks to escape, while the Killer utilizes unique abilities to hunt them down. The **Bite by Night Logo** often appears during loading screens and match transitions, serving as a reminder of the high stakes involved in every round.

| Feature | Survivor/Class Role | Killer Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Objective** | Repairing systems and opening exits. | Preventing escapes and sacrificing players. |
| **Abilities** | Class-specific perks (e.g., healing, speed). | Character-specific powers (e.g., luring, jumpscares). |
| **Victory Condition** | At least one survivor escapes the map. | Eliminating all survivors before they escape. |

## Confirmed Killers: The Faces of Terror

The roster of killers is perhaps the most anticipated aspect of the game. Featuring animatronics from both the mainline FNaF series and popular fan-made titles, the variety of playstyles is immense. The **Bite by Night Logo** is frequently paired with teaser images of these mechanical monsters to build community excitement.

### Ennard: The Amalgamation
Ennard, originating from *FNaF: Sister Location*, is a confirmed killer. As an amalgamation of Funtime animatronics, Ennard brings a unique "body-horror" element to the game. Their lore involves luring Michael Afton into the scooping room to take his place in the world, a narrative thread that may influence their in-game abilities.

### Springtrap: The Relentless Pursuer
Springtrap remains one of the most feared entities in the FNaF universe. In Bite by Night, he is expected to be a high-pressure killer, utilizing his decayed state and mechanical strength to corner survivors. 

### Monster Rat: The Living Nightmare
Interestingly, the wiki notes a common misconception: Monster Rat is not an animatronic but a living creature. This distinction adds a layer of biological horror to the roster, diversifying the threats players will face.
