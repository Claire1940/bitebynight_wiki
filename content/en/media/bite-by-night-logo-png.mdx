## Key Objectives: Generators and Batteries

To win a match in Bite by Night, survivors must focus on the primary objectives. This usually involves repairing generators or finding and installing batteries to power the exit gates. The "Battery" mechanic is particularly intense, as it requires survivors to carry a heavy item that can be "camped" by the Killer.

As seen in competitive play, Killers will often prioritize the battery location to prevent the survivors from skipping time or opening the gates. This creates a "loop" where survivors must juke the killer around obstacles like coffee tables or tent posts to create an opening for their teammates.

### Objective Checklist for Survivors
*   **Locate Generators:** Spread out across the map to avoid being cornered.
*   **Manage Stamina:** Do not run unless the Killer is actively chasing you; you don't want to be "out of gas" during a chase.
*   **Use the Environment:** Look for "soft breakable" walls and pallets to create distance.
*   **Communicate:** If you are playing a Sentinel, let your team know when you have successfully parried the Killer.

## Confirmed Killers: Springtrap and Ennard

The roster of Bite by Night features some of the most feared entities in the FNaF universe. Each Killer brings a different playstyle to the match, requiring survivors to adapt their strategies on the fly.

### Springtrap
Springtrap is a master of area denial. His ability to set traps makes him a nightmare in tight corridors and "dead zones"—areas of the map with no pallets or windows. His axe swing has a significant reach, and he can punish survivors who fail to time their parries correctly.

### Ennard
Ennard, originating from *FNaF: Sister Location*, is a confirmed upcoming killer. As an amalgamation of Funtime animatronics, Ennard is expected to have stealth-based or transformation-based abilities. The lore of Ennard "taking someone's place" suggests that deception may play a role in their gameplay loop.

### Monster Rat
While Monster Rat is currently an unconfirmed killer, the community is highly anticipating his arrival. It is important to note that Monster Rat is considered a "living creature" rather than an animatronic, which may lead to unique biological abilities compared to the mechanical nature of the other killers.

> **Warning:** Avoid getting stuck in corners or "dead ends." Maps in Bite by Night are designed with several bait rooms that lead to no escape, making it easy for a Killer to secure a flawless victory.
