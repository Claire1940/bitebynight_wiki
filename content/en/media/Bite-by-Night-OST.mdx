## How the OST Enhances Gameplay Mechanics

The **Bite by Night OST** is not just a background element; it is a core gameplay mechanic. The game utilizes a "Dynamic Music System" that layers different stems of a track based on the player's current state. 

1. **The Stealth Phase:** While hiding or moving slowly, the music consists of low-frequency drones and occasional metallic pings. This keeps the player on edge without being distracting.
2. **The Detection Phase:** When an animatronic spots the player, a "stinger"—a sudden, sharp musical chord—plays, immediately transitioning the ambient track into a high-energy chase theme.
3. **The Escape Phase:** As the player gains distance, the layers of the **Bite by Night OST** begin to peel away, with the percussion fading out first, signaling that the immediate threat has passed.

> **Tip:** Listen for the "low-end hum" in the soundtrack. Often, a slight change in the bass frequency indicates that an enemy has entered the room, even if you cannot see them yet.
