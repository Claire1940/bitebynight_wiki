## The Definitive Springtrap Skins List

Springtrap is the undisputed star of the current roster, boasting the most intricate skins in the game. In *Bite by Night*, Springtrap's lore deviates slightly from the core FNAF timeline: after being found in the remains of Fazbear Frights, he was upgraded with smarter tech before escaping into the woods. 

His gameplay revolves around his iconic fire axe—a weapon popularized by fan songs and other horror titles like *Dead by Daylight*—which he can throw at survivors. Below is the breakdown of his available cosmetics.

### Ghost of Slaughter (Kratos Reference)
The Ghost of Slaughter skin is a fan-favorite that originated from community artwork on the official Discord. It transforms Springtrap into a robotic version of Kratos from the *God of War* series. This skin replaces the standard fire axe with the Leviathan Axe, complete with a clean, metallic finish. When using this skin, your chase theme shifts to a cinematic orchestral score reminiscent of modern PlayStation titles.

### Toon Trap (Pymations Reference)
If you spent any time in the FNAF fandom circa 2015, you likely remember the "5 AM at Freddy's" series by Pymations. The Toon Trap skin brings that specific, expressive version of Springtrap to life. 
*   **Voice Acting:** This skin features exclusive lines from the original voice actor, Zach X Fuller.
*   **Weapon Swap:** Instead of an axe, Toon Trap wields a crowbar—a direct reference to the "get crowbarred" meme from the animation.
*   **Animations:** His movements are more erratic and "cartoony" compared to the lumbering default model.

### The Hoax (Purple Guy Myth)
The Hoax skin is a deep cut for longtime fans, referencing the fake "Purple Guy in FNAF 2" images that circulated in 2014. It uses a recolored Golden Freddy model as its base, giving it a distinct silhouette compared to other Springtrap skins. It features a custom win animation set in a replica of the FNAF 2 Parts & Service room and plays "It's Been So Long" by The Living Tombstone during the Last Man Standing phase.

## Character Abilities and Skin Variations

While skins in *Bite by Night* are primarily cosmetic, they often change the visual effects of your abilities. For Springtrap, his kit changes entirely depending on whether he is holding his weapon or not.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When you throw your axe (Remnant Cleaver), you lose access to your bear traps until you retrieve it. Make sure your traps are set before engaging in a ranged attack.

### Springtrap Ability Kit
1.  **Bear Trap (Armed):** Places a mechanical trap on the floor. Skins like Toon Trap may feature slightly different trap models.
2.  **Remnant Cleaver (Armed):** A ranged throw. The projectile changes based on the skin (e.g., the Leviathan Axe for Ghost of Slaughter).
3.  **Scream (Unarmed):** Springtrap peels back his mask and yells, obscuring survivor vision. This is a reference to the rare FNAF 3 start-up screens.
4.  **Charge (Unarmed):** A high-speed dash that allows you to grab and slam survivors.

## Other Characters: Mimic and Ennard

While Springtrap currently dominates the **bite by night all skins list**, the developers are actively expanding the roster for other animatronics like The Mimic and Ennard. As of 2026, these characters have fewer skins but feature highly distinct voice lines and mechanical themes.

*   **The Mimic:** Focuses on deception and "compliance" themed dialogue. His lines often sound like automated corporate notices, such as "Continued resistance will result in disciplinary action."
*   **Ennard:** A tangled mess of wires with a focus on stealth and tracking. Ennard's skins are expected to focus on the different animatronic masks he wears throughout the series.

> **💡 Tip:** Keep an eye on the "Last Man Standing" music. Every premium skin has a unique track that plays when only one survivor remains, which can help you identify which killer you're facing even in the dark.

## How to Unlock New Skins in 2026

Unlocking items from the **bite by night all skins list** typically requires in-game currency earned through matches or participating in limited-time events. 

1.  **Match Completion:** Earn "Remnant" by playing as both survivors and killers. High-performance games as the killer reward significantly more currency.
2.  **Achievement Rewards:** Certain skins, like the default variations, are unlocked by reaching specific level milestones with a character.
3.  **Community Events:** Skins like the Ghost of Slaughter were added due to community engagement. Following the official [Bite by Night Roblox Page](https://www.roblox.com/) is the best way to stay updated on new drops.
4.  **Shop Rotation:** The in-game store rotates daily. If a specific skin like "Hoax" isn't available, check back after the server reset.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Bite by Night free to play?**

A: Yes, the official version of *Bite by Night* on Roblox is completely free to play. Be cautious of "scam" games that use the same name but charge Robux for entry; always use the link provided by the official developers.

**Q: Does the bite by night all skins list include stat boosts?**

A: No. All skins are strictly cosmetic to ensure game balance. While they change animations, hitbox sizes and movement speeds remain consistent with the default character models to keep the competition fair.

**Q: Who voices the Toon Trap skin?**

A: Toon Trap is voiced by Zach X Fuller, the same voice actor who played Springtrap in the famous Pymations "5 AM at Freddy's" YouTube series. This makes it one of the most authentic "fan-service" skins in the game.

**Q: How do I change my chase music?**

A: Chase music is tied directly to the skin you have equipped. For example, equipping the Ghost of Slaughter skin will automatically change your chase theme to the God of War-inspired track. There is currently no option to mix and match music between different skins.
