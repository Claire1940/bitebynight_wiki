## Maintaining Your Collection in 2026

To keep your **Bite by Night Shirt** in pristine condition, follow these expert maintenance steps. Proper care ensures that the "custom" feel of the fabric remains intact for years, preventing it from becoming just another "crappy cotton" rag.

*   **Turn Inside Out:** Always turn the shirt inside out before washing to protect the screen-printed logos or "Team Night Bite" graphics.
*   **Cold Wash Only:** Use cold water cycles to preserve the integrity of the cotton fibers and prevent premature shrinking.
*   **Air Dry Recommended:** While the shirts are dryer-safe, air-drying helps maintain the "fluffy" texture mentioned in the original brand lore.
*   **Avoid Bleach:** Even on white shirts, harsh chemicals can break down the specialized fibers used in the 2026 production runs.
