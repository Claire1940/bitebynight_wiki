## Core Gameplay Mechanics

The gameplay of *Bite By Night* revolves around a timed round system. Survivors are dropped into a map—typically a derelict pizzeria or industrial facility—and must complete objectives to "secure" the location.

1.  **Objective Completion**: Technicians must interact with various panels and machinery around the map.
2.  **Resource Management**: Items like alcohol for wounds and batteries for flashlights or tasers must be found and used sparingly.
3.  **Stealth and Evasion**: Using the environment to hide from the killer is often more effective than direct confrontation.
4.  **Last Man Standing**: If the majority of the team falls, the remaining survivor may receive specific buffs or a final chance to escape.

The "Heat" mechanic mentioned in the trailer transcript ("Heat. Heat. Yeah. Heat.") suggests that environmental temperature or machinery heat levels might be a secondary threat or a requirement for completing objectives. Managing these levels while being hunted adds a layer of complexity not seen in standard survival horror titles on [Roblox](https://www.roblox.com).
