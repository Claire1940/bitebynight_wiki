## Springtrap's Upcoming Skin Roster

Springtrap remains one of the most popular Killers in the game, and his skin collection is expanding rapidly in 2026. Developers have confirmed several new looks that pay homage to both the original franchise lore and popular community hoaxes. The diversity in these designs ranges from high-fidelity horror to stylized, cartoony aesthetics.

### The Purple Guy Skin
Based on the classic "hoax" animatronic that circulated in the early days of horror gaming, the Purple Guy skin is a massive, purple animatronic. It features void-black eye holes with piercing white pupils and a signature golden badge. One of the most exciting features of this skin is its custom chase theme, which utilizes the **EGODEATH** motif, a direct nod to IDUTSHANE’s battle theme.

### Toon Springtrap
For those who prefer a break from the hyper-realistic gore, the Toon Springtrap skin offers a "5 AM at Freddy's" aesthetic. This variant replaces Springtrap’s traditional axe with a crowbar and features a significantly lighter color palette. Long-time fans will be pleased to know that **Zachtothefuller**, the original voice actor from the sequel video, provides the voice lines for this specific skin.

## How to Unlock Exclusive Bite by Night Skins

Unlocking **Bite by Night Skins** is rarely a simple matter of spending currency. Many of the most prestigious skins require players to complete specific, high-skill challenges within the "Last Man Standing" (LMS) mode. These challenges often require a deep understanding of the different character classes, particularly the Sentinel class.

### The RustTrap Challenge
To earn the **RustTrap** skin, a heavily withered and broken version of Springtrap, players must follow a specific set of steps during a live match:
1. Enter a Last Man Standing match against a Springtrap player.
2. Select the **Sentinel** class.
3. Successfully stun the Springtrap Killer **5 times** during the match.
4. Survive until the timer runs out.

Failure to complete any of these steps in a single match will reset your progress for the skin. This makes RustTrap one of the more difficult "achievement-based" cosmetics to obtain in the current meta.

## Unconfirmed and Fan-Inspired Designs

One of the most unique aspects of the **Bite by Night Skins** development process is the inclusion of community concepts. The "Ghost of Slaughter" skin, which reimagines Springtrap as Kratos from the *God of War* series, began as fan art by a user named dinahareh on Discord. After catching the attention of the developer OzelBlox, it was officially integrated into the game.

### The Mikutrap Mystery
One of the most discussed unconfirmed skins is **SpringMiku** (or Mikutrap). Based on the Vocaloid idol Hatsune Miku, this skin would feature Springtrap wearing the iconic twin-tails and teal outfit. While it remains unconfirmed, the developers have hinted at more "crossover" style skins if the licensing permits.

### Ignited Springtrap
Based on the Halloween edition of *The Joy of Creation*, the Ignited Springtrap skin is currently listed as "possible" by the lead developer. This skin would feature a missing jaw and glowing orange eyes, providing a much darker and more withered aesthetic than the base model.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Unconfirmed skins are subject to change or cancellation. Do not spend in-game credits on "leaked" bundles until they appear in the official store or patch notes.

## FAQ

**Q: Are Bite by Night Skins purely cosmetic?**

A: Yes, all skins are designed to be cosmetic and do not provide statistical advantages such as increased movement speed or damage. However, some skins like Toon Springtrap change your weapon model (e.g., crowbar instead of axe), which can slightly alter your visual perspective.

**Q: How do I participate in skin concept contests?**

A: The developers frequently monitor the official Discord and Twitter (X) accounts for fan art. The Ghost of Slaughter skin is a prime example of a community design being added to the game after gaining significant traction online.

**Q: Can I unlock the Purple Guy skin right now?**

A: As of March 2026, the Purple Guy skin is listed as an upcoming release. Keep an eye on the in-game store and the "Achievements" tab for the specific unlock mission once the patch goes live.

**Q: Will there be more crossover Bite by Night Skins in the future?**

A: While the developers have expressed interest in crossovers (like the Kratos-inspired skin), they have stated that these are "possible" but depend on community interest and licensing. The SpringMiku skin is currently the most requested crossover variant.
