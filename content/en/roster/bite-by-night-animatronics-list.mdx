## Springtrap: The Relentless Trapper

Springtrap is the entry point for most players on the **bite by night animatronics list**. Despite being free, he is a high-tier threat in the right hands due to his dual-mode gameplay. His kit revolves around his Remnant Cleaver, which completely changes his ability set depending on whether he is holding it or has thrown it.

### Springtrap Abilities
*   **Swing (M1):** Deals 34 damage while holding the axe. If the axe is thrown, damage drops to 28.
*   **Remnant Cleaver (Q):** Springtrap throws his axe, dealing 19 instant damage and applying a 15-second bleed effect (35 total damage).
*   **Bear Trap (E):** Places a trap that stuns survivors for 10 seconds. This is the ultimate tool for cutting off loops and door-spamming.
*   **Scream (E - No Axe):** Replaces the trap when the axe is thrown. Blinds survivors and reveals their locations while regenerating 25 stamina.
*   **Charge (R - No Axe):** A high-speed rush that, if successful, triggers a cutscene dealing 40% of the survivor's current HP.

Springtrap excels at mid-range pressure. By throwing the cleaver, you not only track the survivor but also unlock the **Charge** ability, which is one of the most effective gap-closers in the game.

## Mimic: The Versatile Shapeshifter

The Mimic is widely considered the most adaptable killer on the **bite by night animatronics list**. With the highest health pool in the game and three distinct modes, the Mimic can change its strategy mid-chase to counter any survivor tactic.

### Mimic Modes and Mechanics
The Mimic uses **Mode Switch** to cycle through three states:

1.  **Strength Mode:** This is the default state. It provides the highest M1 damage in the game (44 damage per swing) but offers no special movement abilities.
2.  **Speed Mode:** Increases movement speed by 15% at the cost of 75% damage reduction. This mode unlocks **Leap**, allowing the Mimic to jump vertically or horizontally to bypass obstacles.
3.  **Stealth Mode:** The most powerful state. The Mimic makes no noise, and while damage is reduced by 25%, it unlocks the **Grab** and **Drill** abilities.

### Key Ability: Drill
The Drill ability allows the Mimic to submerge underground, becoming invisible and revealing all survivor locations. When emerging, it deals 38 AoE damage. This is the premier tool for map pressure, as it allows you to teleport across the map and ambush survivors working on generators without warning.

## Ennard: The Master of Deception

Ennard is the most expensive character currently on the **bite by night animatronics list**, and for good reason. His kit is designed to counter the "looping" meta where survivors use doors and windows to stay away from the killer indefinitely.

### Ennard's Kit
*   **Wire Eyes (Q):** Ennard throws minions that roam and reveal players. If they land on a survivor, they block the screen, deal 16+ damage, and slow the survivor down.
*   **Grab (E):** Extends wire arms to pull a survivor directly to Ennard, setting up a guaranteed M1 swing.
*   **Skin Stealer (R):** This is Ennard's signature move. He can take the form of a survivor to blend in. While in this form, he can use **Skinstealer Stab** (45 damage) or **Skinstealer Explode** (25 AoE damage).

> **⚠️ Warning:** When Ennard kills a player while using Skin Stealer, the death notification is hidden from other survivors, making it easy to pick off a team one by one.

## Strategic Overview: Which Killer Should You Choose?

Choosing from the **bite by night animatronics list** depends entirely on your preferred playstyle. 

*   **For Beginners:** Stick with **Springtrap**. His bear traps are easy to place at doorways, and his axe throw provides a forgiving way to deal damage from a distance.
*   **For Aggressive Players:** Choose the **Mimic**. The ability to switch to Speed Mode to catch up and then Strength Mode to deliver a 44-damage finishing blow is incredibly satisfying.
*   **For Tactical Players:** **Ennard** is the choice. His ability to impersonate survivors allows for "social deduction" style gameplay, where you can walk right up to a generator and wipe out a group before they realize you aren't an ally.

Regardless of your choice, remember that map knowledge is king. Knowing where generators are located and which doors can be destroyed will make any animatronic on this list significantly more dangerous.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the strongest killer on the bite by night animatronics list?**

A: While subjective, most high-level players rank **Ennard** as the strongest due to his anti-looping "Wire Eyes" and his ability to hide kills using "Skin Stealer." However, the **Mimic** is a close second for pure damage and map mobility.

**Q: How do I earn Scraps quickly to unlock new animatronics?**

A: Scraps are earned by completing objectives as a survivor (repairing generators) or by successfully damaging and sacrificing survivors as a killer. Playing as Springtrap in the early game is the most efficient way to farm Scraps for the Mimic or Ennard.

**Q: Can survivors see the Mimic when he is using the Drill ability?**

A: No, the Mimic is completely invisible while underground. However, survivors should listen for a distinct mechanical rumbling sound, which indicates the Mimic is nearby and preparing to sprout.

**Q: Does Springtrap's Bear Trap stay on the map forever?**

A: Springtrap can have up to three Bear Traps active at once. If he places a fourth, the oldest one will disappear. Additionally, if Springtrap uses his Remnant Cleaver and does not retrieve it, his trap ability is temporarily replaced by his Scream ability.
