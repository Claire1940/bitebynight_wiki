## The Marionette: The Sentinel of Lost Souls

Often referred to as the Puppet, the Marionette is one of the most anticipated additions to the **Bite by Night upcoming killers** list. Lore-wise, this entity is possessed by Charlotte Emily and serves as a protector for other spirits. In-game, the Marionette will likely function as a high-pressure killer that forces survivors to multitask.

The most defining feature of the Marionette is its **Music Box dependency**. Unlike other killers that can be stopped by physical barriers, the Marionette is kept in a neutralized state as long as a music box is wound. Once the music stops, the Marionette can float through the environment, ignoring standard physical limitations to find its prey.

### Abilities and Counters
*   **Levitation and Flight:** The Marionette does not walk; it glides through the air at high speeds, making it difficult to track via footsteps.
*   **Perception Influence:** It can cause the screens of survivors to flicker or distort, creating a sense of panic and making it harder to complete objectives.
*   **The Music Box:** Survivors must constantly interact with the music box. If it runs out, the Marionette becomes an unstoppable force that cannot be blocked by doors.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not let the music box reach zero. The Marionette is the only killer that completely ignores door mechanics once its "trance" is broken.

## Withered Foxy: The Aggressive Hallway Stalker

Following the Marionette, Withered Foxy is expected to bring a more direct, high-speed threat to the game. As a heavily damaged version of the original pirate fox, this killer relies on raw agility and sudden lunges. 

Withered Foxy's gameplay revolves around the **Hallway Stalker** mechanic. He will frequently appear at the end of long corridors, preparing to charge. Survivors will need to be vigilant with their flashlights, as light is his primary weakness.

| Mechanic | Description | Survivor Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hallway Stalking** | Appears at the end of hallways to prepare a lunge. | Constant visual checks of long corridors. |
| **Light Sensitivity** | Stunned or repelled by bright, focused light. | **Strobe** the flashlight (rapidly flash) to reset his AI. |
| **Extreme Speed** | Moves faster than almost any other starting killer. | Listen for the mechanical "sprinting" audio cue. |

## Mangle: The Ceiling Ambush Specialist

Mangle represents a significant shift in how survivors must observe their environment. While most killers move along the floor, Mangle utilizes **Wall and Ceiling Crawling** to bypass obstacles and enter rooms from unexpected angles. This makes Mangle one of the most dangerous **Bite by Night upcoming killers** for players who forget to look up.

Mangle also emits a loud, distorted **Radio Interference** noise. While this serves as a warning that the killer is nearby, the static is intentionally designed to drown out other audio cues, such as the footsteps of other killers or the sounds of nearby objectives being completed.

### Key Strategies for Mangle
1.  **Check the Vents and Ceilings:** Mangle will rarely approach through a standard doorway.
2.  **Filter the Static:** Learn to distinguish the specific frequency of Mangle's radio noise to determine its distance.
3.  **Monitor the Monitors:** Mangle is highly agile and can traverse the entire map in seconds; keeping tabs on its location via cameras is vital.

## Nightmare Freddy and the "Fifth Killer" Poll

In a recent community poll conducted by the developers, Nightmare Freddy emerged as the clear favorite for the "fifth" major update. This version of Freddy Fazbear is a manifestation of trauma and utilizes a unique **Hive Mind** mechanic involving "Freddles."

Nightmare Freddy does not hunt in the traditional sense. Instead, he spawns three smaller versions of himself (Freddles) that slowly gather on a survivor's position. If all three Freddles manifest, they summon the full-sized Nightmare Freddy for a lethal jumpscare. Survivors must use their flashlights to scare away the Freddles before they can complete the summoning ritual.

> **💡 Tip:** If you hear high-pitched screeches, a Freddle has spawned nearby. Use your light immediately to force it to retreat.

## The Impact of Copyright and Rebranding

An important note for fans following the development of these killers is the recent shift in naming conventions. Due to copyright discussions with the original creator of the Five Nights at Freddy's franchise, the developers have renamed several assets to ensure the game can remain monetized and active on platforms like [Roblox](https://www.roblox.com).

While the mechanics of the **Bite by Night upcoming killers** will remain the same, their names and models may undergo slight variations to avoid direct infringement. For example:
*   **Springtrap** is now **The Rotten**.
*   **Ennard** is now **The Doppelganger**.
*   **The Mimic** is now **The Mistake**.

The developers are currently in talks to reach an agreement that preserves the spirit of the characters while respecting intellectual property laws. This rebranding ensures that the game has a long-term future and that the upcoming roster can be released without legal interruptions.
