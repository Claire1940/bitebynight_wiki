## Complete Survivor Roles and Stats

Survivors in Bite By Night are categorized by their utility, health, and speed. While every survivor shares a baseline for movement, their unique abilities define their role within a team. Currently, players can choose from four distinct roles, ranging from the basic Customer to the high-tech Security Guard.

| Role | Health | Cost (Scraps) | Primary Utility | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Customer** | 100 | **Free** | Self-Sustain/Speed | Easy |
| **Medic** | 90 | **400** | Team Healing | Easy |
| **Fighter** | 100 | **600** | Stunning/Combat | Hard |
| **Security Guard** | 100 | **900** | Intel/Stunning | Medium |

### The Customer (Starter Role)
The Customer is the default role for every new player. While it is often considered "bare bones" by veteran players, it offers surprisingly good survivability for those who know how to manage their resources.

*   **Pizza (Q):** Consuming pizza replenishes **20% of your total health**. This is a great tool for recovering after a narrow escape.
*   **Drink (E):** Provides a significant speed boost and reduces stamina consumption for a short duration. However, be warned: once the effect wears off, you suffer a **slowness debuff** and slower stamina regeneration.

> **💡 Tip:** Only use the Drink ability when you are certain you can reach a safe zone or a hiding spot before the slowness debuff kicks in.

### The Medic (Support Role)
At 400 Scraps, the Medic is the most affordable unlockable survivor. Despite having slightly lower health (90 HP), the Medic is vital for long-duration matches where chip damage from the killer adds up.

*   **Heal Others (Q):** Restores 5 HP every 2 seconds to a teammate.
*   **Self-Heal (E):** Restores 5 HP every 1 second to yourself. This is significantly faster than the team heal but has a 60-second cooldown.

### The Fighter (Combat Role)
The Fighter is the only role capable of directly confronting an animatronic to buy time for the team. It is widely considered the hardest role to master due to its reliance on timing.

*   **Swing (Q):** Deals a 3-second stun to the animatronic.
*   **Parry (E):** A defensive stance that stuns the attacker for 2 seconds and adds a "charge" to your axe. Successfully parrying is the only way to regenerate your Q ability charges.

### The Security Guard (Intel Role)
The Security Guard is currently the most expensive and arguably the most powerful survivor in the **bite by night all characters list**. 

*   **Taser (Q):** A projectile stun that grows stronger the longer you charge it. A full charge can immobilize a killer for a significant duration.
*   **Cams (E):** Access the facility's camera system to highlight the killer, other survivors, and generators. This essentially provides the team with legal "wall hacks" to track the killer's movements.

## The Animatronics: Killer Abilities Explained

The "Killers" in Bite By Night are based on iconic animatronics. Each has a unique playstyle, ranging from trap-setting to shapeshifting. Understanding how these entities operate is essential whether you are playing as them or trying to escape them.

### Springtrap (The Hunter)
Springtrap is the free starter killer. He excels at area denial and tracking wounded survivors.

*   **Axe Throw (Q):** Throwing the axe marks any survivor it hits, allowing you to see their silhouette through walls. You must manually pick the axe back up (F) to use certain other abilities.
*   **Trap (E - with Axe):** Place a floor trap that roots survivors in place.
*   **Scream (E - without Axe):** If your axe is currently thrown, your E ability changes to a Scream that temporarily blinds nearby survivors.

### Mimic (The Specialist)
Costing 4,500 Scraps, the Mimic is a versatile killer that can swap between three different modes: **Strength, Speed, and Stealth**.

| Mode | Key Feature | Unique Ability |
|---|---|---|
| **Strength** | High Damage | Heavy Strike |
| **Speed** | High Mobility | Lunge/Leap |
| **Stealth** | Low Terror Radius | Burrow (Travel Underground) |

The Stealth mode is widely considered the most effective for high-level play, as it allows the Mimic to traverse the map undetected and ambush survivors working on generators.

### Ennard (The Amalgamation)
Ennard is the most expensive animatronic at 6,000 Scraps. His gameplay revolves around deception and crowd control.

*   **Grab (E):** Ennard uses his wires to pull a survivor toward him, closing the gap instantly.
*   **Shapeshift (R):** Ennard can take the form of any survivor currently in the lobby. This allows you to approach generators and "help" survivors before revealing your true form and attacking.

## Advanced Gameplay Strategies for 2026

To succeed in Bite By Night, you must look beyond individual abilities and focus on team composition and map awareness. 

### Survivor Team Synergy
A balanced team is significantly more likely to escape. Experts recommend the following composition for a 4-player squad:
1.  **The Security Guard:** Stays near the center of the map, calling out the killer's location via cameras.
2.  **The Fighter:** Acts as the "distractor," leading the killer away from generators and using parries to stay alive.
3.  **The Medic:** Follows the Fighter or stays near the generators to keep the team at high health.
4.  **The Customer/Second Medic:** Focuses purely on completing objectives (generators) while using speed boosts for quick rotations.

### How to Earn Scraps Fast
Since the best characters in the **bite by night all characters list** require thousands of Scraps, optimizing your earnings is vital.
*   **Focus on Objectives:** Completing generators provides a steady stream of Scraps.
*   **Survivor Saves:** Rescuing teammates from hooks or traps offers a significant bonus.
*   **Last Man Standing:** Even if your team loses, being the last survivor alive grants a multiplier to your end-match rewards.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid "tunneling" or chasing a single survivor for too long as a killer. Efficient survivors will finish three generators while you are distracted by one Fighter.

## FAQ

**Q: Who is the best character on the bite by night all characters list?**

A: For survivors, the **Security Guard** is currently considered the best due to the utility of the Cams and Taser. For killers, the **Mimic** in Stealth mode offers the highest win rate for experienced players.

**Q: How do I regenerate the Fighter's stun ability?**

A: You must use the **Parry (E)** ability to successfully block an animatronic's attack. Each successful parry adds a charge back to your Swing (Q) ability.

**Q: Can Ennard stay in survivor form indefinitely?**

A: While Ennard can stay transformed for a long time, attacking or taking damage will usually force him back into his animatronic form. Use the disguise to get close to unsuspecting groups.

**Q: Does the Medic's self-heal have a limit?**

A: There is no limit to how many times you can use it per match, but it is governed by a **60-second cooldown**. It is best saved for situations where you have escaped a chase but are at critical health.
