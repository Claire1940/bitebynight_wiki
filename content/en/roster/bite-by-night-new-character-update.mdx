The most vocal part of the community has long debated the power of the Security Guard. In the **bite by night new character update**, the developers have finally addressed the "stun-lock" meta that made playing as a killer frustrating. As of the 2026 update, once you are out, your voice is silenced to living players, heightening the tension and isolation of the survivors.

### New Win Conditions and Time Penalties
The developers have increased the stakes for every kill. When a killer successfully eliminates a survivor, the match timer now drops by **25 seconds** (up from 20 seconds). This puts immense pressure on the remaining survivors to complete objectives quickly. 

1.  **Kill Penalty:** Each death reduces the remaining time by 25 seconds.
2.  **Base Time Scaling:** When fewer survivors are left, the game sets a base time to prevent matches from dragging on indefinitely.
3.  **Red Outlines:** Killers should now see survivors outlined in red under specific conditions, though early reports suggest this visual cue is subtle.
