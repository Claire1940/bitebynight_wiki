## The 2026 Copyright Shift: Why Names Changed

One of the most significant changes to occur in April 2026 is the sudden renaming of several iconic killers. Many players initially feared that Scott Cawthon, the creator of the FNAF franchise, had issued a formal cease-and-desist order. However, the reality is more nuanced and reflects the developers' desire to ensure the long-term survival of the game.

The developers are currently in a proactive dialogue with the FNAF legal team. To minimize the risk of a copyright strike while these negotiations are ongoing, the team decided to change the names of the killers. This allows them to keep the popular models in the game while showing "good faith" to the original IP holder. The goal is to eventually secure official permission to use the models and names, potentially leading to a more formal collaboration.
