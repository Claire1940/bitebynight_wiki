## Mastering the Animatronics

Playing as the Killer requires a shift in mindset. You aren't just a hunter; you are a tactician. Currently, the roster features several powerful entities, each with a unique playstyle.

### The Rotten (Springtrap)
The Rotten is the most balanced choice for beginners. His kit revolves around area denial and consistent damage.
*   **M1 (Left Click):** A lunge forward with an axe swing (approx. 28 damage).
*   **Q Ability:** Throws the axe. This inflicts bleed damage, slowly draining the survivor's health. You must manually pick the axe back up after throwing it.
*   **E Ability:** Bear Traps. These are essential for "Agony" level play. Placing them in bottlenecks or near the exit gate can catch survivors for up to 5 seconds.

### Advanced Killers
1.  **The Mistake:** Focuses on mobility and teleportation. High skill ceiling but very rewarding for map pressure.
2.  **The Doppelganger:** Can mimic dead players to trick survivors. This is highly effective against inexperienced groups but loses value against coordinated teams who track the kill feed.

> **⚠️ Warning:** As a Killer, do not spam your attack. There is a 2-second cooldown on swings. If you miss, you give the survivor a massive window to escape or stun you.

## How to Farm Scrap Efficiently

Scrap is the lifeblood of progression in Bite by Night. You need it to unlock better roles and more powerful animatronics. To maximize your earnings in 2026, follow these methods:

1.  **Fix Every Generator:** As a survivor, you earn scrap for every percentage of progress you contribute to a generator.
2.  **Secure Kills:** As an animatronic, every elimination grants a significant scrap bonus. A full server wipe can net you upwards of 400 scrap in a single match.
3.  **Survive to 6 AM:** The survival bonus is substantial. Even if you didn't do much work on generators, simply making it to the exit gate pays out well.

## The Agony of the Endgame: 6:00 AM Tactics

When the clock hits 5:00 AM, the game enters its most intense phase. The exit gates become the focal point of the map. As a survivor, you should have saved your stamina and health items for this moment. 

As a Killer, this is where you "camp" the storage area or the known exit points. Placing traps around the exit gate is a common but effective strategy. If you are playing as the **Security Guard**, use your cameras to see which gate the survivors are congregating at so you can intercept them.

## Advanced Combat Tips

Whether you are using a taser or an axe, combat in Bite by Night is about timing and spacing. 

*   **Switching Targets:** As an animatronic, don't get hyper-focused on one "looper." If a survivor is leading you away from a nearly finished generator, break off the chase and protect the objective.
*   **Stamina Management:** Never let your stamina bar hit zero. If you are exhausted, your regeneration rate is significantly penalized, making you an easy target for the Rotten's axe throw.
*   **Trap Rotation:** Traps do not last forever. They eventually despawn. If you are playing a long match, remember to circle back and re-trap high-traffic areas.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best survivor role in this bite by night agony guide?**

A: For beginners, the **Medic** is the best role because it allows you to recover from mistakes. For advanced players, the **Security Guard** provides the best utility for the entire team by tracking the Killer's movements.

**Q: How do I get my axe back as The Rotten?**

A: After using your Q ability to throw the axe, you must walk over to where it landed and interact with it to pick it up. You do significantly less damage with your bare hands.

**Q: Does the timer speed up?**

A: Yes. Every time survivors complete a generator or submit a battery, the clock jumps forward by approximately one hour. Completing objectives quickly is the fastest way to reach the 6:00 AM endgame.

**Q: Can survivors break through barricaded doors?**

A: No, survivors cannot break through doors barricaded by their teammates. However, the animatronic can eventually break them down, though it takes time and leaves them vulnerable to stuns.
