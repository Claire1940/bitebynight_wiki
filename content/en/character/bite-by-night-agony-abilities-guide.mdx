## Animatronic Ability Breakdown

Playing as an animatronic (the killer) is often considered the more straightforward path for beginners, but high-level play requires precise timing. Most killers share a basic control scheme but possess unique secondary and tertiary powers that define their "Agony" level on the map.

### The Rotten (Springtrap)
The Rotten is the quintessential starter killer, offering a balanced kit that punishes survivors for poor positioning. His primary weapon is an axe, which provides both melee and ranged pressure.

| Ability | Input | Effect | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Axe Swing** | M1 (Left Click) | Forward lunge with a heavy axe strike. Deals ~28 damage. | 2 Seconds |
| **Axe Throw** | Q Key | Throws the axe as a projectile. Inflicts **Bleed** damage over time. | Variable (Must pick up) |
| **Bear Trap** | E Key | Places a trap with a visible trigger radius. Snares for 5 seconds. | 15 Seconds |

> **💡 Expert Tip:** Do not spam your M1 click. The 2-second cooldown is punishing if you miss. Instead, use your Axe Throw to initiate a chase and force the survivor into a Bear Trap you've placed at a bottleneck.

### The Mistake and The Doppelganger
For players looking for more "Agony" in their gameplay, advanced animatronics offer supernatural utility.

*   **The Mistake:** This killer focuses on mobility. His primary draw is the ability to teleport across short distances, allowing him to bypass barricades and surprise survivors fixing generators.
*   **The Doppelganger:** A psychological powerhouse. The Doppelganger can "steal" the appearance of a dead player. While this rarely tricks veteran players who check the leaderboard, it is devastating in uncoordinated lobbies or during the chaotic final minutes of a round.

## Strategic Map Interaction

To succeed in the current 2026 meta, you must understand the environment. Both killers and survivors need to interact with the map's unique features to gain an advantage.

### Barricades and Choke Points
Survivors can barricade doors by holding the interaction prompt and keeping a cursor within a moving circle. This is a vital mechanic for stopping a chase. However, killers can break through these with repeated M1 attacks. 

As a killer, you should memorize the "bottlenecks"—areas like the storage room entrance or narrow corridors leading to the central generator hub. Placing Bear Traps here is significantly more effective than placing them in open rooms.

### The 6:00 AM Rush
When the clock hits 6:00 AM, the dynamics of the game shift instantly. The survivors must reach the exit (usually located in the storage/loading dock area).
1.  **Killers:** Move toward the exit immediately. Camping the exit is a controversial but effective strategy to catch survivors who managed to stay hidden all night.
2.  **Survivors:** Save your stamina for this final sprint. If you are playing as a Security Guard, use your cameras one last time to see which path the killer is patrolling before making your break for it.

## Earning and Spending Scrap

Scrap is the lifeblood of Bite by Night. You earn it by fixing generators, submitting batteries, and—if you're the killer—securing eliminations. 

*   **Generator Completion:** Yields the highest scrap for survivors.
*   **Eliminations:** Killers earn a significant bonus for every player removed from the game.
*   **Winning the Round:** A successful 6:00 AM escape or a total team wipe can net you upwards of 400 scrap in a single match.

Spend your scrap in the shop to unlock higher-tier roles like the **Fighter** or the **Security Guard**. These roles offer much higher survival rates in competitive lobbies compared to the free Customer role. ## FAQ

**Q: What is the most effective way to use the bite by night agony abilities guide for beginners?**

A: Focus on mastering "The Rotten" first. His bear traps are the most reliable way to secure kills without needing perfect aim. For survivors, stick to the Medic role so you can recover from mistakes while learning the map layout.

**Q: Does the Axe Throw ability permanently remove your weapon?**

A: No, but it does leave you vulnerable. After throwing the axe with the Q key, you must physically walk over to where it landed to retrieve it. While your axe is gone, your melee damage is significantly reduced.

**Q: How do I counter the Doppelganger's mimicry?**

A: Always keep an eye on the player list. If you see a "player" walking toward you but their name is listed as dead on the UI, it is the Doppelganger. Additionally, the Doppelganger often lacks the specific animations of the role they are mimicking (like the Medic's healing glow).

**Q: Can multiple survivors fix the same generator?**

A: Yes, and it is highly recommended. Multiple survivors on a single generator will complete the task faster, which in turn reduces the overall game timer by one hour for every completed objective set.
