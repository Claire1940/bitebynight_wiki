Understanding how this animatronic navigates the facility is the first step toward survival. He utilizes the vent system and the main corridor with terrifying efficiency.

In the 2026 version of the game, his AI level scales based on the current hour. During the early game, he acts as a scout, testing your reaction times. By 3 AM, his aggression spikes, and he begins to utilize "stutter-stepping," a mechanic where he moves twice in a single AI tick.

## The Revenge Mechanic and "Give Me Back My Face"

This state is often triggered if the player successfully wards him off three times in a single hour without checking his starting location. During this phase, Bonnie becomes significantly more vocal, often emitting distorted audio that sounds like a plea for his missing features.

This mechanic is designed to punish players who play too defensively. If you notice the lights in the office dimming permanently, you have entered the Revenge window. During this time, Bonnie's movement speed increases by 40%, and he may bypass the vent lights entirely, appearing directly in the center of the room.

## Resource Management and Power Conservation

Managing your power is crucial when dealing with **bite by night withered bonnie**. Every time you use the vent lights or the hallway flash to deter him, you consume a percentage of your remaining energy. In the later nights of 2026 gameplay, power efficiency is the difference between winning and losing.

2. **Camera stalling:** If you see Bonnie on Cam 08, do not switch cameras. Keeping the feed on him can occasionally "stall" his movement for several seconds.

## FAQ

**Q: Why does bite by night withered bonnie keep jumpscaring me even when I have the mask on?**

A: This usually happens because the mask was put on too late. In the 2026 version, you must have the mask fully equipped before the lights finish flickering. If Bonnie has already reached the center of the office, the mask will no longer work.

**Q: Can you prevent Withered Bonnie from leaving Parts & Service?**

A: No, you cannot permanently trap him. However, frequently checking the Parts & Service camera during the first hour can delay his initial departure by up to 30 seconds.

**Q: What is the "Give me back my face" audio trigger?**

A: This is a rare audio cue that signals Bonnie has entered "Revenge Mode." When you hear this, his AI difficulty effectively jumps by 5 points for the remainder of the night, and you must use the light-reset tactic mentioned earlier in this guide.

**Q: Does Withered Bonnie move on Night 1?**

A: In most standard configurations of Bite by Night, Bonnie remains inactive on Night 1 to allow the player to learn the basic controls. However, in "Aggressive Mode" settings, he can become active as early as 2 AM on the first night.
