## How to Unlock the Security Guard Class

Before you can start stunning animatronics and monitoring the feeds, you must unlock the class through the in-game shop. Unlike basic survivor roles, this specialized class requires a specific amount of currency earned through gameplay.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Shop Category** | Survivors |
| **Unlock Cost** | **900 Scraps** |
| **Primary Role** | Tactical Support / Staller |
| **Difficulty** | Moderate |

To acquire the class, navigate to the main menu, enter the shop, and select the "Survivors" tab. Once you have accumulated 900 scraps, you can permanently add the guard to your roster. Note that scraps are earned by completing generators, surviving chases, and successfully escaping matches, so consistent play is rewarded.

## Mastering the Bite by Night Security Guard Abilities

The strength of this class lies in its two unique active abilities: the Taser and the Camera System. While other classes might focus on repair speed or stealth, the **Bite by Night Security Guard** is designed to interact directly with the threat and the environment.

### 1. The Taser (Stun Mechanic)
The Taser is your primary defensive tool. It allows you to fire a projectile that stuns or significantly slows down animatronics. The most critical aspect of this tool is the "Charge" mechanic. The longer you hold the fire button, the more potent the electrical discharge becomes.

| Charge Level | Effect on Animatronic | Tactical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quick Fire** | Minor Slowdown | Emergency self-defense during a close chase. |
| **Medium Charge** | Brief Stun | Stopping a killer from breaking a barricade. |
| **Full Charge** | Significant Stun / Stop | Saving a teammate who is about to be caught. |

> **💡 Tip:** Do not waste your Taser shots at extreme distances. While it has a decent range, the projectile has a travel time, and missing a shot leaves you vulnerable while the cooldown resets.

### 2. Camera Surveillance (Intel Gathering)
Information is often more valuable than raw power. By accessing the "Cams" ability, you can look through various fixed cameras positioned around the map. This allows you to track the killer's movement without putting yourself in physical danger.

*   **Reveal Animatronics:** Clicking on a killer while viewing them through a camera will highlight their position for your entire team.
*   **Identify Generators:** You can use the cameras to locate unfinished generators, helping your team coordinate their repair efforts more efficiently.
*   **Safety Check:** Before entering a high-traffic area like the main hallway, check the nearby camera feed to ensure the coast is clear.

## Tactical Gameplay Strategies

Playing as a **Bite by Night Security Guard** requires a shift in mindset. You are not just a generator technician; you are the team's eyes and ears. To maximize your effectiveness, implement the following strategies during your matches.

### The Art of the Stall
When a killer is chasing a teammate, your job is to intervene. Use the Taser to interrupt the chase. If the killer is busy breaking a barricade or a door, use that time to fully charge your Taser. Releasing a full charge just as they finish the animation will frustrate the killer and give your teammate enough time to reach a new loop or hiding spot.

### Stamina Management
The Security Guard does not have infinite endurance. Because you will often be in the "danger zone" to land Taser shots, you must monitor your stamina bar closely. Never deplete your stamina entirely while trying to line up a shot. If you miss, you need enough remaining energy to sprint to the nearest window or pallet.

### Strategic Camera Usage
Do not spend too much time on the cameras. While the intel is vital, a survivor sitting in a corner on their tablet is a survivor who isn't fixing generators. Use the cameras in short bursts:
1.  Check the killer's location.
2.  Tag them for the team.
3.  Exit the feed and return to an objective.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be aware of your surroundings while using cameras. You are completely blind to your immediate environment while looking at the feeds, making you an easy target for stealthy animatronics.

## Team Synergy and Composition

The **Bite by Night Security Guard** performs best when paired with classes that focus on objective speed. By keeping the killer distracted and revealed, you allow your "Engineers" or "Repair Specialists" to finish generators without fear of a surprise attack.

| Synergy Partner | Why it Works |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Engineer** | You stall the killer while they finish high-speed repairs. |
| **Medic** | You protect the Medic while they heal downed teammates. |
| **Scout** | Combined with your cameras, the killer will almost always be visible on the map. |

When playing with a coordinated group, designate one person to stay near the **Bite by Night Security Guard** for protection. Having a second player nearby to drop pallets or provide a distraction if your Taser is on cooldown creates a "loop" that is very difficult for killers to break.

## Survival Tips for 2026

1.  **High Ground Advantage:** Use cameras to monitor different floors. If you see the killer taking the stairs, you can meet them at the top with a pre-charged Taser.
2.  **Generator Baiting:** Start a generator to lure the killer, then hide nearby. When they arrive to kick the generator, hit them with a Taser and rotate to a different objective.
3.  **End-Game Protection:** Once the exit gates are powered, your Taser becomes the most important tool in the game. Stand near the gate switches and keep the killer at bay while your teammates work the lever.
4.  **Audio Cues:** Don't rely solely on cameras. Listen for the heavy footsteps or mechanical whirring of the animatronics. The cameras should confirm what your ears are already telling you.

The role of the **Bite by Night Security Guard** is one of high risk and high reward. By mastering the timing of your Taser and the strategic placement of your surveillance, you become the backbone of any successful survival attempt.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the Security Guard worth 900 scraps?**

A: Yes, the **Bite by Night Security Guard** is widely considered one of the best support classes in the game due to its ability to both stun killers and provide map-wide intel. It is a high-priority unlock for players who enjoy tactical gameplay.

**Q: Can the Taser run out of ammo?**

A: The Taser operates on a cooldown system rather than limited ammunition. After firing, you must wait for the device to recharge before it can be used again. Managing this cooldown is essential for survival.

**Q: Does the "Reveal" mechanic work through walls?**

A: Once you click on an animatronic through the camera system, their silhouette or a marker will be visible to your teammates for a short duration, even through walls, allowing for better navigation and avoidance.

**Q: Can the killer disable the cameras?**

A: Some killers have specific abilities that can temporarily jam or disable camera feeds. If you see static on your screen, it is a sign that the killer is nearby or using a disruptive ability, and you should relocate immediately.
