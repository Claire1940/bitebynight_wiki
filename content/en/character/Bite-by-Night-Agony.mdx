## What is Bite by Night Agony?

In the world of Bite by Night, Agony is more than just a number on your screen; it is a thematic representation of the haunting energy that powers the animatronics. The game utilizes a two-currency system to keep the economy balanced and rewarding for long-term players. While "Malice" is often earned through general participation, Agony is typically reserved for higher-tier unlocks and performance-based rewards.

There are two primary ways the term is used within the game:

1.  **The Meta-Currency:** This is the "Bite by Night Agony" you see in the main menu shop. It is used to purchase legendary skins like the **Anomaly (Ennard)** or the **Molten Freddy** variant. It is also required to unlock specific killers if you didn't obtain them during the initial launch period.
2.  **In-Match Resource:** For killers like The Mimic or Springtrap, "Agony management" refers to how they use their ability cycles. Some high-tier powers require a buildup of Agony during a chase to execute finishing blows or map-wide screams.

### Daily Rewards and Quality of Life Updates
The recent Quality of Life (QOL) update introduced a robust Daily Reward system. Logging in consecutively provides a multiplier to your Agony gains. On the seventh day of a login streak, players often receive a significant "Agony Burst," which can be enough to push you over the threshold for a new character unlock.

### High-Performance Gameplay
Killers earn more Agony by dragging out chases and utilizing their full ability kits rather than simply "camping" a single survivor. For survivors, earning **Bite by Night Agony** is tied to objective completion and "Boldness" points. Successfully parrying a killer as the Fighter class or hitting a perfect "Tug" mini-game on a generator will significantly boost your end-match payout.

> **💡 Tip:** Check the "Achievements & Badges" section in the main menu. Many one-time challenges offer 500+ Agony for tasks like "Stun the Killer 5 times in a single match."

## Tactical Agony Management for Killers

For those playing the killer role, **Bite by Night Agony management** is a core gameplay mechanic. Your most powerful abilities are often gated behind an Agony meter that fills as you interact with survivors.

For example, when playing as **The Mimic**, switching between Strength, Speed, and Stealth modes consumes a small portion of your internal Agony. If you deplete this resource by spamming mode switches, you may find yourself unable to use your "Dig" ability when you actually need to cut off a survivor at a loop.

### Balancing Ability Usage
*   **Don't Over-Reveal:** Using Springtrap's reveal scream is tempting, but if you already have a survivor in your sights, save the Agony for your bear trap placements.
*   **The Disguise Cost:** As Ennard, maintaining a survivor disguise slowly drains your meter. Use the disguise to get close to a generator, then strike quickly to conserve your resources for the next chase.
*   **The Rage Meter:** Some concepts currently being tested in the community wiki suggest a "RAGE" meter that fills when you are stunned. This is a secondary form of in-match Agony that allows for "The Unforgiving" state—a massive buff to speed and damage.

## Survivor Strategies for the 2026 Meta

Survivors don't have an "Agony meter" to manage in-match, but their actions directly influence the killer's ability to gain it. A smart survivor team can effectively "starve" a killer of their resources by playing cautiously.

### Class-Specific Tips
*   **The Security Guard:** Use your taser sparingly. A missed taser shot doesn't just leave you vulnerable; it gives the killer a "Confidence" boost that can accelerate their Agony gain.
*   **The Fighter:** Your fire axe is a powerful tool, but every successful parry feeds into the killer's Rage. Only engage in combat when necessary to protect a teammate who is nearly finished with a generator.
*   **The Healer:** Staying hidden is your best bet. By preventing the killer from landing hits, you deny them the Agony they need to activate their map-wide abilities.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful with the "Last Man Standing" mechanic. When only one survivor remains, the killer often receives a massive Agony infusion, making them faster and more lethal for the final showdown.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I buy Bite by Night Agony with Robux?**

A: While the primary way to earn Agony is through gameplay, there are typically "Agony Boosters" available in the Roblox store that increase your earn rate for a set number of matches. Direct purchases of large Agony bundles are occasionally available during seasonal events.

**Q: Does Agony reset every season?**

A: No, your accumulated **Bite by Night Agony** is a permanent currency that stays with your account across updates. However, new seasonal "Malice" tracks may be introduced that require separate progression.

**Q: What is the fastest way to earn Agony as a beginner?**

A: Focus on completing your Daily Rewards and playing the "Security Guard" class. The Guard earns bonus points for spotting the killer on cameras, which provides a steady stream of Agony even if you aren't great at winning chases yet.

**Q: Is there a cap on how much Agony I can hold?**

A: There is currently no known hard cap on **Bite by Night Agony**, allowing dedicated players to save up for multiple upcoming killer releases or legendary skin drops.
