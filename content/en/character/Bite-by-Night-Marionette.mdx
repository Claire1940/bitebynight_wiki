## Appearance and Visual Design

The visual design of the **Bite by Night Marionette** stays true to its iconic roots while receiving a high-fidelity polish suitable for a 2026 Roblox experience. The model features a slender, elongated black body that emphasizes its supernatural and unsettling nature. According to official sneak peeks and wiki stubs, the character's limbs are adorned with white horizontal stripes, a classic aesthetic that contrasts sharply with the dark environments of the game's maps.

The most striking feature of the Marionette is its face—a white comedy mask that hides the spirit within. These tears are a direct nod to the character's tragic end and its eternal sorrow. Additionally, the chest of the animatronic features three distinct white buttons, rounding out a design that is both simplistic and terrifying.

## Predicted Gameplay Mechanics

While specific abilities for the **Bite by Night Marionette** have not been fully disclosed, we can extrapolate potential mechanics based on the character's history and the "DBD-inspired" nature of the game. In most iterations of the Puppet, a "Music Box" is a central theme. It is highly probable that the Marionette will feature a map-wide objective that survivors must manage to prevent the killer from gaining a massive speed boost or an instant-down capability.

### Potential Ability: The Lullaby's Curse
The Marionette might project a "Terror Radius" that sounds like a distorted music box. If survivors stay within this radius for too long without completing a specific interaction (like "winding" a station), they might suffer from the *Powerless* debuff, making them easier to track or capture.

### Potential Ability: Strings of Fate
Given the character's name, a "string" mechanic could allow the Marionette to traverse the map vertically or "tether" survivors to a specific area, preventing them from reaching exit gates or windows during a high-stakes chase.
